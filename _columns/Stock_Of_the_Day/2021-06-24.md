Singapore-based **Kulicke & Soffa Industries** ([KLIC](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=KLIC)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/), breaking out of a cup-with-handle base in heavy volume Thursday. KLIC stock is up 86% this year.




The company designs and manufactures equipment used to assemble semiconductor devices for the automotive, consumer, communications, computing and industrial markets.


KLIC stock rose 6.6% to 59.14 on the [stock market today.](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/) The buy point starts at 57.60 out of a [cup-with-handle](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/common-patterns-1/) pattern and tops out at 60.48.


Kulicke & Soffa is the top-ranked stock in IBD's Electronics-Semiconductor Equipment industry group. It's followed by **Applied Materials** ([AMAT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AMAT)) and **ASML Holding** ([ASML](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ASML)). The industry group is ranked No. 57 out of 197 groups that IBD tracks.

Breakout Follows Beat-And-Raise Earnings Report
-----------------------------------------------


On May 5, Kulicke & Soffa easily beat analyst expectations for its [fiscal second quarter](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/klic-stock-news-kulicke-and-soffa-industries-beats-fiscal-q2-targets/) and guided higher for the current period. But KLIC stock dropped 10% after the report.


Revenue jumped 126% to $332 million from the year-ago period. Adjusted earnings soared 385% to $1.26 a share in the quarter ended April 3.


After hitting a low of 43.41 in mid-May, the stock is up 36%.


The semiconductor market is a key sector to watch because chips play a vital role in new technologies. Computer chips are the enabling technology behind a host of emerging trends, from the Internet of Things and 5G wireless to self-driving cars and artificial intelligence.


KLIC Stock Has A Strong Composite Rating
----------------------------------------


The [IBD Stock Checkup tool](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-kulicke-and-soffa-ind-inc-klic.aspx) shows that KLIC stock has a best-possible [IBD Composite Rating](https://education.investors.com/financial-dictionary/general/composite-rating-cma--smartselect-rgr-) of 99.
