**Epam Systems** ([EPAM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=EPAM)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the tech services company continues to post steady growth despite the economic upheaval caused by the coronavirus pandemic. Epam stock rose on Tuesday.




The Newtown, Pa.-based company provides software development and digital platform engineering services to 120 Fortune Global 2,000 customers. Its customers cover a host of industries including financial services, travel and consumer, software and high tech, and business information and media.


Epam stock has formed a [three-weeks-tight pattern](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-the-3-weeks-tight-pattern-gives-you-an-extra-buy-point/) with a [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) of 339.01, according to [IBD MarketSmith charts](https://www.investors.com/product/marketsmith/?artProdLink=MarketSmith). In a bullish sign, Epam stock found support at its [10-week moving average line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-buy-stocks-why-the-10-week-moving-average-offers-new-entry-points/) during the recent sell-off in tech stocks.


On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/), Epam stock climbed 2.4% to close at 321.73.

Relative Strength Line Near High
--------------------------------


Another positive for Epam stock is that its [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/a-stock-breakout-specialty-tool-the-relative-strength-line/) is near an all-time high as it outperforms the broader market. The relative strength line compares a stock's performance to that of the S&P 500.


Typically, relative strength lines for the strongest stocks either confirm or lead a stock's price into new high ground. The RS line is in blue on all IBD stock charts, including those on MarketSmith.


On April 28, Epam stock broke out of a [cup-with-handle base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/the-basics-how-to-analyze-a-stocks-cup-with-handle/) at a buy point of 211.57, according to MarketSmith charts. It marched progressively higher after its [breakout](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-stock-breakout/), reaching a record high 338.91 on Sept. 2.



Epam On Eight-Quarter Winning Streak
------------------------------------


Epam Systems beat Wall Street's sales and earnings targets over the last eight quarters. Most recently, it delivered upbeat results for the second quarter on Aug. 6. Epam earned an adjusted $1.46 a share, up 14% year over year, on sales of $632.4 million, up 15%. Analysts had expected earnings of $1.21 a share on sales of $597.3 million in the June quarter.


"Epam's better-than-expected second-quarter performance demonstrates a continued need for our services during a time when businesses had to quickly pivot and adapt to an unpredictable environment," Chief Executive Arkadiy Dobkin said in a news release.


The Covid-19 pandemic forced many of its customers to accelerate their digital transformation projects, Dobkin said on a conference call with analysts.
