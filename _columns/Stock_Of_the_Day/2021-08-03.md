The SPDR S&P Homebuilders ETF ([XHB](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=XHB)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day.](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) The housing ETF is offering an early buying opportunity as homebuilding and related stocks such as **Lennar** ([LEN](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=LEN)), **Home Depot** ([HD](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HD)) and **Floor & Decor** ([FND](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FND)) break out or set up again.




U.S. homebuilding increased more than expected in June, according to data from the Commerce Department, as city dwellers look for [more spacious housing outside cities](https://www.investors.com/news/moving-during-covid-turns-into-great-migration-americans/) amid the Covid-19 pandemic. Low-interest rates have also fueled housing demand.


The pandemic has also boosted home improvements projects with home offices and larger outdoor space.


With raw materials on a roller-coaster ride this year, an exchange traded fund is a good way for investors to take advantage of multiple segments of an industry, while also reducing the risks associated with investing in a single stock.


Many housing stocks have consolidated for a few months and are moving higher again.

Stock Of The Day In Buy Range
-----------------------------


The XHB ETF rose 1.65% to 76.33 in the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/product/marketsmith/?artProdLink=MarketSmith). XHB is consolidating in a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/flat-base-stock-dollar-tree-breakout/) with an 80.92 buy point, according to [MarketSmith analysis](https://www.investors.com/product/marketsmith/?artProdLink=MarketSmith). Investors also have an early buying opportunity now as the ETF bounces above its 50-day/10-week line. The ETF is currently 4.5% above the 10-week line.


A decisive close below the 10-week line would be a sell signal, signifying a loss of roughly 6%.



Housing Stocks Step Up
----------------------


XHB is known as the homebuilding ETF. But the fund [includes more than just homebuilders](https://www.investors.com/etfs-and-funds/etfs/best-etfs-do-you-really-know-whats-inside-your-etf-probably-not/) and building product providers. Home furnishing providers, home improvement stores and household appliance makers make up a large chunk of the ETF's nearly $2 billion in assets.


Floor & Decor stock is the top holding in the ETF. The specialty flooring provider and recent [IBD Stock of the Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/fnd-stock-breaks-out-into-buy-zone-as-housing-starts-lift-floor-and-decor/) broke out of a cup-with-handle base last month. FND stock is extended, with earnings due on Thursday.


Another holding, mattress maker **Tempur Sealy** ([TPX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TPX)) is in [buy range](https://www.investors.com/research/the-new-america/tpx-stock-rises-as-wellness-boom-boosts-mattress-demand/) after breaking out of a flat base.


Among other holdings within the ETF, Home Depot stock is nearing its [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) of 333.55 as it consolidates in a cup-with-handle base.


Among homebuilders within the fund, **NVR** ([NVR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVR)) is hovering around a buy point. Lennar stock, a recent Stock Of The Day, is moving up the right side of cup base with a 110.71 buy point. **PulteGroup** ([PHM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=PHM)) is also consolidating.
