**New Oriental Education & Technology** ([EDU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=EDU)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/). New Oriental Education stock has new base and buy point. The China stock has been a top performer in the highly rated Consumer Services-Education Group, soaring 98% so far this year.




New Oriental Education is the largest provider of private educational services in China. [According to New Oriental](http://investor.neworiental.org/corporate-profile), the company has a network of 95 schools and about 8.4 million enrollments in fiscal year 2019 ended May 31.


New Oriental Education Stock Analysis
-------------------------------------


Shares of the [IBD 50](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/ibd-50/) member rose 1.85% to 108.40 on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/). New Oriental Education stock is back above a [three-weeks-tight](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-the-3-weeks-tight-pattern-gives-you-an-extra-buy-point/) buy point of a 108.10 entry. The three weeks tight is an opportunity for existing holders to buy a few extra shares.


But New Oriental Education stock also now has a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-patterns-flat-base-dull-trade-positive-action/) on the daily charts, [MarketSmith analysis shows](https://marketsmith.investors.com/). That buy point is 110.



Both of these entries are above the 92.95 buy point that the China stock cleared after breaking out from a [cup with handle base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/the-cup-with-handle/). New Oriental Education hit a record 109.10 on June 23 following its latest quarterly report. The opening price of 105 from that earnings gap-up also was a legitimate entry.


The [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/a-stock-breakout-specialty-tool-the-relative-strength-line/) for the [IBD Leaderboard](https://leaderboard.investors.com/#/leaders/leadersnearabuypoint) stock has been rising strongly in 2019, and is right at all-time highs.


The China stock boasts a perfect [IBD Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/find-evaluate-stocks/exclusive-ratings/) of 99. The [Stock Checkup Tool](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-new-orientl-eduandtech-ads-edu.aspx) shows New Oriental Education earnings solid with 20% average annual growth over the past three years, with revenue gains of 30%. However, New Oriental Education earnings growth has cooled from 56% to 21% and 9% in the most recent quarters. But analysts expect New Oriental Education earnings to rise 32% this year and next year.


Top China Stocks In Education
-----------------------------


New Oriental Education is one of the [top Chinese stocks](https://www.investors.com/news/best-chinese-stocks-to-buy-and-watch/) to watch in today's stock market. Its 98% gain in 2019 is far higher than the 30% run enjoyed by its rival stock **TAL Education** ([TAL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TAL)). Last month the latter firm posted [weaker-than-expected earnings](https://www.investors.com/news/china-stocks-new-oriental-education-earnings-tal-education-earnings-q2-2019/), while New Oriental's results were mixed.


TAL Education stock rose just over 3% to 35.09 Tuesday, just below its 50-day line. TAL shares have a new consolidation with a 39.76 buy point.


Several U.S. education stocks also are doing well, including **Chegg** ([CHGG](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CHGG)). The Consumer Services-Education group ranks No. 20 out of the 197 industries that IBD tracks.


Analyst Backs China Stock
-------------------------


Goldman Sachs analyst Christine Cho reiterated her buy rating and 121 price target for New Oriental Education stock on July 30. She said she continues "to see a favorable setup" for fiscal 2020. She highlighted the firm's increased focus on operational efficiency and the newer learning centers added in the last two years.
