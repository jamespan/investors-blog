**Celestica** ([CLS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CLS)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the stock rockets after the Canadian company's beat-and-raise third-quarter earnings report.  
**Celestica** （[CLS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CLS)） 是 [IBD 今日最佳股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为在这家加拿大公司发布第三季度收益报告后，该股飙升。


The Toronto-based electronics contract manufacturer [late Wednesday](https://corporate.celestica.com/news-releases/news-release-details/celestica-announces-third-quarter-2024-financial-results-and) said adjusted earnings rose 60% year over year to $1.04 a share. Sales climbed 22% to $2.5 billion. Analysts polled by FactSet had expected earnings of 93 cents a share on sales of $2.41 billion.  
这家总部位于多伦多的电子合同制造商[周三晚些时候](https://corporate.celestica.com/news-releases/news-release-details/celestica-announces-third-quarter-2024-financial-results-and)表示，调整后收益同比增长 60% 至每股 1.04 美元。销售额增长 22% 至 25 亿美元。接受 FactSet 调查的分析师此前预计每股收益为 93 美分，销售额为 24.1 亿美元。




For the current quarter, Celestica expects to earn $1.04 a share, up 37%, on sales of $2.5 billion, up 17%. That's based on the midpoint of its outlook. Wall Street was predicting earnings of 95 cents a share on sales of $2.45 billion for the fourth quarter.  
对于本季度，Celestica 预计每股收益为 1.04 美元，增长 37%，销售额为 25 亿美元，增长 17%。这是基于其前景的中点。华尔街预测第四季度的销售额为 24.5 亿美元，每股收益为 95 美分。


Celestica also provided its outlook for next year. For 2025, it forecast adjusted earnings of $4.42 a share, up 15% from its target of $3.85 for this year. It sees revenue climbing 8% to $10.4 billion from its 2024 target of $9.6 billion.  
Celestica 还提供了对明年的展望。到 2025 年，它预测调整后每股收益为 4.42 美元，比今年的目标 3.85 美元增长 15%。它预计收入将从 2024 年的 96 亿美元目标增长 8% 至 104 亿美元。


In afternoon trading on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/), Celestica stock jumped nearly 16% to 66.30.  
在[今天](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/)股市的下午交易中，Celestica 股票上涨近 16% 至 66.30。


With the move, Celestica stock gapped out of a [consolidation pattern](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/shopify-stock-consolidation-pattern-led-to-beautiful-gains/) at a [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) of 63.49, according to [IBD MarketSurge](https://get.investors.com/marketsurge/?src=apa1bq) charts. Celestica had tested that buy point for three trading sessions starting Oct. 11 but closed each of those days below the buy point.  
根据 [IBD MarketSurge](https://get.investors.com/marketsurge/?src=apa1bq) 图表，随着此举，Celestica 股票在[买入点](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) 63.49 跳空摆脱[盘整模式](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/shopify-stock-consolidation-pattern-led-to-beautiful-gains/)。从 10 月 11 日开始，Celestica 已经测试了该买入点位，但这些交易日的收盘价都低于买入点位。


AI, Cloud Computing Driving Celestica Sales人工智能、云计算推动 Celestica 销售
------------------------------------------------------------------


Celestica is an electronics contract manufacturer serving information technology, communications, aerospace and defense, health tech and other industries.  
Celestica 是一家电子合同制造商，服务于信息技术、通信、航空航天和国防、健康科技和其他行业。


On a conference call with analysts, Chief Executive Rob Mionis said the company's third-quarter outperformance was driven by "continued strong demand" in the company's Connectivity and Cloud Solutions group.  
在与分析师的电话会议上，首席执行官 Rob Mionis 表示，该公司第三季度的出色表现是由公司连接和云解决方案集团的“持续强劲需求”推动的。


The company also announced a new business relationship with artificial intelligence services firm Groq. Celestica is supporting Groq in the manufacturing of servers for AI and machine learning as well as full rack solutions.  
该公司还宣布与人工智能服务公司 Groq 建立新的业务关系。Celestica 正在支持 Groq 制造用于 AI 和机器学习的服务器以及全机架解决方案。


Groq has developed a proprietary silicon platform known as the Language Processing Unit, which specializes in accelerated inferencing.  
Groq 开发了一个专有的芯片平台，称为 Language Processing Unit，专门用于加速推理。


Celestica Stock Gets Price-Target HikesCelestica 股票的目标价上调
---------------------------------------------------------


At least three Wall Street analysts raised their price targets on shares after the company's earnings report.  
在公司财报发布后，至少有三名华尔街分析师上调了他们的股票目标价。


RBC Capital Markets analyst Paul Treiber reiterated his outperform rating on Celestica stock and upped his price target to 75 from 65.  
RBC Capital Markets 分析师 Paul Treiber 重申了他对 Celestica 股票的跑赢大盘评级，并将目标价从 65 上调至 75。


"Celestica is seeing increasing traction at hyperscalers and a mix shift to higher-margin programs," Treiber said in a client note.  
“Celestica 看到超大规模公司的吸引力越来越大，并且正在向更高利润的项目转变，”Treiber 在一份客户报告中说。


BMO Capital Markets analyst Thanos Moschopoulos maintained his outperform rating on Celestica stock and increased his price target to 72 from 64.  
BMO Capital Markets 分析师 Thanos Moschopoulos 维持对 Celestica 股票的跑赢大盘评级，并将目标价从 64 上调至 72。


"We believe the AI capex cycle, and Celestica's market position, will be more durable than the stock's current valuation implies — and see further upside to the stock, particularly given Celestica's consistent margin execution," Moschopoulos said in a report.  
Moschopoulos 在一份报告中表示：“我们相信 AI 资本支出周期和 Celestica 的市场地位将比该股目前的估值所暗示的更持久——并看到该股的进一步上涨空间，特别是考虑到 Celestica 始终如一的利润率执行。
