**Goldman Sachs** ([GS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=GS)) is Monday's [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the Dow and S&P 500 banking stock eyes a buy point just above a key trading level amid its 55% advance this year.  
**高盛** （[GS）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=GS) 是周一的 [IBD 今日股票](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为道琼斯指数和标准普尔 500 银行股在今年上涨 55% 的情况下，将买入点视为略高于关键交易水平的买入点。


Morgan Stanley analyst Betsy Graseck on Monday upped the price target on Goldman Sachs shares to 736, up from 570. That analyst expects President-elect Donald Trump to create a favorable environment for large-cap banking stocks.  
摩根士丹利分析师 Betsy Graseck 周一将高盛股票的目标价从 570 上调至 736。该分析师预计当选总统唐纳德·特朗普将为大型银行股创造有利的环境。


Part of that will be higher capital-markets activity, and a "more favorable regulatory framework" likely to encourage mergers and acquisitions along with IPOs.  
其中一部分将是更高的资本市场活动，以及可能鼓励并购和 IPO 的“更有利的监管框架”。




"While the (Republican) sweep also brings with it tariff and immigration changes, the size and impact to the economy is unknown today," Graseck added.  
“虽然（共和党）大获全胜也带来了关税和移民的变化，但其规模和对经济的影响今天尚不清楚，”格拉塞克补充道。


With the fourth quarter fast coming to an end, analysts project EPS growing 49% with revenue rising 7%. For the full year, the consensus puts Goldman Sachs profit up 60% to $36.63 per share on sales totaling $51.71 billion, a 12% gain compared with 2023. Analysts expect 2025 earnings per share of $42.74 and revenue coming in at $54.94 billion.  
随着第四季度即将结束，分析师预计每股收益将增长 49%，收入增长 7%。全年，市场普遍认为高盛利润增长 60% 至每股 36.63 美元，销售额总计 517.1 亿美元，与 2023 年相比增长 12%。分析师预计 2025 年的每股收益为 42.74 美元，收入为 549.4 亿美元。


On Oct. 15, the global investment bank reported Q3 earnings of $8.40 per share to easily top FactSet expectations for $6.89. Net revenue rose 7% to $12.7 billion, well above expectations for $11.77 billion.  
10 月 15 日，这家全球投资银行报告称，第三季度每股收益为 8.40 美元，轻松超过了 FactSet 预期的 6.89 美元。净收入增长 7% 至 127 亿美元，远高于预期的 117.7 亿美元。


Net interest income rose to $2.62 billion. Goldman Sachs recorded a 20% increase in investment banking revenue while investment management revenue rose 10%. The bank increased its provision for credit losses to $397 million, up from $282 million in Q2 and $7 million last year, respectively.  
净利息收入增至 26.2 亿美元。高盛的投资银行业务收入增长了 20%，而投资管理收入增长了 10%。该银行将其信贷损失准备金分别从第二季度的 2.82 亿美元和去年的 700 万美元增加到 3.97 亿美元。

GS shares edged down 0.9% to 594.12 during Monday's [market action](https://www.investors.com/category/market-trend/stock-market-today/). The stock has forged a [three-weeks-tight](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-the-3-weeks-tight-pattern-gives-you-an-extra-buy-point/) pattern, according to [MarketSurge charts](https://marketsurge.investors.com/). The traditional tight [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/apple-stock-set-up-proper-buy-point-before-big-rally/) is 612.73, the all-time high that GS hit on Nov. 29.  
在周一的[市场走势](https://www.investors.com/category/market-trend/stock-market-today/)中，GS 股价小幅下跌 0.9% 至 594.12。根据 [MarketSurge 图表](https://marketsurge.investors.com/)，该股已经形成了[为期三周的窄幅](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-the-3-weeks-tight-pattern-gives-you-an-extra-buy-point/)模式。传统的紧[买点](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/apple-stock-set-up-proper-buy-point-before-big-rally/)是 612.73，这是 GS 在 11 月 29 日创下的历史新高。



Shares are finding support just above the stock's [21-day exponential moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/). Year to date, Goldman Sachs stock has gained 55%. That includes a 17.5%  jump in November.  
股价在该股的 [21 天指数移动平均线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/)上方找到支撑。年初至今，高盛股票上涨了 55%。这包括 11 月份 17.5% 的增长。


Last week, Keefe Bruyette analyst David Konrad raised the firm's price target on Goldman Sachs to 686, up from 570, maintaining an outperform rating on the shares. The U.S. bank stocks have had a strong run following better-than-expected earnings and, more important, the election results, the analyst tells investors in a research note.  
上周，Keefe Bruyette 分析师 David Konrad 将该公司对高盛的目标价从 570 上调至 686，维持对该股的跑赢大盘评级。这位分析师在一份研究报告中告诉投资者，在好于预期的收益以及更重要的是选举结果之后，美国银行股表现强劲。


Bruyette wrote that there are "potential tangible outcomes" postelection of Donald Trump to drive higher earnings, including investment banking, trading and market-sensitive revenues like custody and asset management.  
布鲁耶特写道，唐纳德·特朗普当选后有“潜在的切实成果”来推动更高的收益，包括投资银行、交易和市场敏感的收入，如托管和资产管理。


On Nov. 25, HSBC analyst Saul Martinez downgraded Goldman Sachs to hold from buy, and increased the price target to 608 from 569. Martinez said he remains positive on the fundamental outlook for Goldman. However, he added that investors should be wary of arguments that an investment banking supercycle is set to drive shares much higher.  
11 月 25 日，汇丰银行分析师 Saul Martinez 将高盛的评级从买入下调至持有，并将目标价从 569 上调至 608。马丁内斯表示，他对高盛的基本前景仍然持乐观态度。然而，他补充说，投资者应该警惕有关投资银行超级周期将推动股价大幅走高的论点。


The Dow banking stock has a robust 91 [Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stocks-to-buy-and-watch-ibd-composite-rating-top-growth-stocks/). Goldman Sachs has a 90 [Relative Strength Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/) and an 87 [EPS Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/eps-rating-is-key-to-picking-great-stocks/).  
道琼斯银行股的综合[评级](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stocks-to-buy-and-watch-ibd-composite-rating-top-growth-stocks/)为 91 分。高盛的[相对强弱评级](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/)为 90，[每股收益评级](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/eps-rating-is-key-to-picking-great-stocks/)为 87。
