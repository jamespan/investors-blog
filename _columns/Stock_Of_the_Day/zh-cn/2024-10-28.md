**Nu Holdings** ([NU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NU)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the Brazilian digital banking upstart delivers rapid and profitable growth. Nu stock flashed a bullish signal on Monday as it broke out to a post-IPO high.  
**Nu Holdings** （[NU）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NU) 是[当今 IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为这家巴西数字银行新贵实现了快速且有利可图的增长。Nu 股票周一闪现看涨信号，突破 IPO 后高点。


Founded in 2016, Nu Holdings says it uses data and technology to transform banking and empower people. It serves more than 105 million customers across Brazil, Mexico and Colombia.  
Nu Holdings 成立于 2016 年，该公司表示，它利用数据和技术来改变银行业并赋予人们权力。它为巴西、墨西哥和哥伦比亚的超过 1.05 亿客户提供服务。


Nu Stock Breaks Out Nu Stock 爆发
-------------------------------


Shares of Nu Holdings popped 3.6% on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/research/stock-market-data-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-spdr-etfs/). That gain bumped Nu stock above a 15.16 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/) from a six-week, first-stage flat base, according to [MarketSurge pattern recognition](https://marketsurge.investors.com). The base rides neatly on support at the chart's 50-day/10-week moving average. Earnings are set for Nov. 13.  
Nu Holdings 的股价[今天在股市](https://www.investors.com/research/stock-market-data-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-spdr-etfs/)上上涨了 3.6%。根据 MarketSurge 模式识别，这一上涨使 Nu 股票从为期六周的第一阶段flat base。该基础巧妙地支撑在图表的 50 天/10 周移动平均线的支撑位上。财报定于 11 月 13 日公布。



The [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/growth-stocks-breakout-specialty-tool-relative-strength-line/) for Nu stock hit a new high as the stock broke out, a positive sign. That is marked by a blue circle at the end of the RS line on the weekly MarketSurge chart. A rising RS line means that a stock is outperforming the S&P 500 index.  
随着股票的爆发，Nu 股票的[相对强弱线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/growth-stocks-breakout-specialty-tool-relative-strength-line/)创下新高，这是一个积极的信号。这在每周 MarketSurge 图表上 RS 线末端的蓝色圆圈中标记。上升的 RS 线意味着一只股票的表现优于标准普尔 500 指数。


Latin American peer **MercadoLibre** ([MELI](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MELI)), an online retail play, also advanced within a buy range. The [IBD 50](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/ibd-50/) and [Leaderboard](https://leaderboard.investors.com/#/leaders/leadersnearabuypoint) stock's buy range runs to 2,121. Earnings are due Nov. 6.  
拉丁美洲同行 **MercadoLibre** （[MELI）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MELI) 是一家在线零售公司，也在买入区间内上涨。[IBD 50](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/ibd-50/) 和 [Leaderboard](https://leaderboard.investors.com/#/leaders/leadersnearabuypoint) 股票的买入区间达到 2,121。财报将于 11 月 6 日到期。




The Global X MSCI Argentina ETF ([ARGT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ARGT)) is on [SwingTrader](https://swingtrader.investors.com/#/). Shares hit a new high on Monday.  
Global X MSCI 阿根廷 ETF （[ARGT）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ARGT) 在 [SwingTrader](https://swingtrader.investors.com/#/) 上。股价周一创下新高。


Besides Nu Holdings, **Affirm** ([AFRM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AFRM)) and **KKR** ([KKR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=KKR)) are among financial stocks currently on the [IBD 50 list](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/ibd-50/) of top growth stocks. Generally solid earnings have boosted the sector.  
除了 Nu **Holdings，Affirm** （[AFRM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AFRM)） 和 **KKR** （[KKR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=KKR)） 目前都在 [IBD 50](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/ibd-50/) 顶级成长型股票名单上的金融股票中。总体而言，稳健的收益提振了该行业。


Rapid Earnings, Revenue Growth For Nu StockNu Stock 的快速收益和收入增长
--------------------------------------------------------------


The [IBD Stock Checkup tool](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-nu-holdings-a-nu.aspx) shows that Nu is currently the No. 1 stock in the foreign bank industry group, in terms of its IBD [Composite Rating](https://education.investors.com/financialdictionary.aspx?mode=1&term=Composite+Rating+SmartSelect&termid=1).  
[IBD 股票检查工具](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-nu-holdings-a-nu.aspx)显示，就其 IBD [综合评级](https://education.investors.com/financialdictionary.aspx?mode=1&term=Composite+Rating+SmartSelect&termid=1)而言，Nu 目前是外国银行行业组的第一只股票。


Nu stock earns a stellar Composite Rating of 98, an RS Rating of 93 and an EPS Rating of 81, all out of a best-possible 99. The EPS score is held back by a relatively short earnings history.  
Nu 股票获得了 98 分的出色综合评分、93 分的 RS 评分和 81 分的每股收益评分，均满分 99 分。EPS 分数受到相对较短的收益历史的阻碍。


The fast-growing digital bank saw some wild swings after its initial public offering in December 2021. But it has delivered positive earnings every quarter since Q3 2022, according to FactSet.  
这家快速增长的数字银行在 2021 年 12 月首次公开募股后出现了一些剧烈波动。但根据 FactSet 的数据，自 2022 年第三季度以来，它每个季度都实现了正收益。


In August, Nu reported that it earned 10 cents per share in the second quarter on sales of $2.85 billion. Year over year, Nu earnings doubled and revenue jumped 52.5%. Over the last three quarters, Nu's earnings growth averaged 161%, according to the Stock Checkup tool.  
8 月，Nu 报告称，第二季度销售额为 28.5 亿美元，每股收益 10 美分。与去年同期相比，Nu 收益翻了一番，收入增长了 52.5%。根据 Stock Checkup 工具的数据，在过去三个季度，Nu 的收益平均增长了 161%。


Analysts project that the company's earnings will surge 74% to 42 cents per share for the full year, rising a further 43% in 2025. Sales are seen rising a 29% in 2024 and 31% in 2025, though below the more robust 67% pace in 2023.  
分析师预计，该公司全年的收益将飙升 74% 至每股 42 美分，到 2025 年将进一步增长 43%。预计 2024 年的销售额将增长 29%，2025 年将增长 31%，但低于 2023 年更强劲的 67% 的速度。


A Cathie Wood And Warren Buffett PickCathie Wood 和 Warren Buffett 的选择
---------------------------------------------------------------------


[Both Warren Buffett and Cathie Wood own Nu stock](https://www.investors.com/stock-lists/ipo-analysis/nu-stock-warren-buffett-cathie-wood/), though they are very different types of investors.  
[沃伦·巴菲特 （Warren Buffett） 和凯西·伍德 （Cathie Wood） 都拥有 Nu 股票](https://www.investors.com/stock-lists/ipo-analysis/nu-stock-warren-buffett-cathie-wood/)，尽管他们是非常不同类型的投资者。


Year to date, Nu stock has jumped 87% to a post-IPO high. That rally could limit upside potential from the latest breakout.  
年初至今，Nu 股票已上涨 87% 至 IPO 后的高点。这种反弹可能会限制最近一次突破的上行潜力。


FactSet shows that 10 of the 21 analysts covering Nu stock rate it a sell or a hold. They have, on average, a price target of 14.56, below where shares were trading on Monday.  
FactSet 显示，在报道 Nu 股票的 21 位分析师中，有 10 位将其评为卖出或持有。平均而言，他们的目标价为 14.56，低于周一的股价水平。
