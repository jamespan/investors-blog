**Samsara** ([IOT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=IOT)) is the [IBD Stock of the Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as shares find support at a key technical level after a big run-up. Samsara stock has gained nearly 50% in 2024.  
**Samsara** （[IOT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=IOT)） 是[当今的 IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为股票在大幅上涨后在关键技术层面获得支撑。Samsara 股票在 50 年上涨了近 2024%。


On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/?), Samsara stock rose 5.6% to close at 50.61. Thursday's gain took out its intraday 52-week high of 50.07.  
[今日股市](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/?)上，Samsara 股价上涨 5.6%，收于 50.61。周四的涨幅突破了 52 周盘中高点 50.07。




Samsara reached that high on Sept. 25. It retreated a bit after that, but found support at its [21-day moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-trade-stocks-using-21-day-exponential-moving-average/).  
轮回在 9 月 25 日达到了这个高点。此后它略有回落，但在 [21 天移动平均线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-trade-stocks-using-21-day-exponential-moving-average/)找到支撑。


Founded in 2015, Samsara provides sensors and cloud-based software to manage vehicle fleets and industrial operations. Also, its Internet of Things platform provides global positioning tracking for trucks, and monitors routes and vehicle performance.  
Samsara 成立于 2015 年，提供传感器和基于云的软件来管理车队和工业运营。此外，其 Internet of Things 平台为卡车提供全球定位跟踪，并监控路线和车辆性能。


Samsara Stock: Founders Samsara 股票：创始人
--------------------------------------


Sanjit Biswas and John Bicket co-founded Samsara in 2015. Earlier they founded Meraki, a Wi-Fi and cloud networking startup. They sold Meraki to **Cisco Systems** ([CSCO](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CSCO)) for $1.2 billion in 2012.  
Sanjit Biswas 和 John Bicket 于 2015 年共同创立了 Samsara。早些时候，他们创立了 Meraki，这是一家 Wi-Fi 和云网络初创公司。2012 年，他们以 12 亿美元的价格将 Meraki 出售给 **Cisco Systems** （[CSCO）。](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CSCO)


Samsara began by selling fleet management software in the transportation industry. By leveraging its Global Positioning System technology along with telematics — sensor data gathered from trucks — Samsara eventually helped customers monitor routes and vehicle performance. That helped them save money on preventive maintenance and fuel usage.  
Samsara 最初在运输行业销售车队管理软件。通过利用其全球定位系统技术以及远程信息处理（从卡车收集的传感器数据），Samsara 最终帮助客户监控路线和车辆性能。这帮助他们节省了预防性维护和燃料使用方面的资金。


From there, Samsara in 2018 expanded into video-based safety products. Further, these enabled fleet customers to lower vehicle insurance costs.  
从那时起，Samsara 于 2018 年扩展到基于视频的安全产品。此外，这些使车队客户能够降低车辆保险成本。


In-cab, dual-facing cameras provide real-time video streaming. Also, artificial intelligence software at cloud data centers detects high-risk behavior, such as distracted driving and lane departure.  
驾驶室内的双面摄像头提供实时视频流。此外，云数据中心的人工智能软件可以检测高风险行为，例如分心驾驶和车道偏离。


Samsara Stock: Asset Tags A Hit
-------------------------------


Many analysts are upbeat about new "asset tags" introduced at an investor conference in June.  
许多分析师对 6 月份投资者会议上引入的新“资产标签”持乐观态度。


BMO Capital Markets analyst Daniel Jester said in a report to clients: "Newly announced Asset Tags extend the company's opportunity beyond vehicle and asset gateways for customers to track a vast assortment of smaller, nonpowered assets ubiquitous inside industrial organizations in a way which will be supportive to Samsara's gross margins."  
BMO Capital Markets 分析师 Daniel Jester 在给客户的一份报告中表示：“新发布的 Asset Tags 将公司的机会扩展到车辆和资产网关之外，让客户可以跟踪工业组织内无处不在的各种小型非动力资产，这将支持 Samsara 的毛利率。


In the quarter ending Aug. 3, the asset tags generated $1 million in annual contract value.  
在截至 8 月 3 日的季度中，资产标签产生了 100 万美元的年度合同价值。


"Assuming an effective price of $5 per device, the $1 million in net new ACV in Q2 implies 200,000 devices sold," said Morgan Stanley analyst Keith Weiss in a report.  
“假设每台设备的有效价格为 5 美元，第二季度 100 万美元的净新 ACV 意味着售出 200,000 台设备，”摩根士丹利分析师 Keith Weiss 在一份报告中表示。


Growing Non-Vehicle Market不断增长的非汽车市场
------------------------------------


William Blair analyst Dylan Becker said in a report: "Samsara continues to see notable momentum across its growing suite of non-vehicle-based offerings like asset tags, equipment monitoring, site visibility, connected workflows, and training. With many customers allocating eight-figures-plus annually toward missing and stolen assets, we believe asset tags provide another opportunity for Samsara to deliver substantial tangible value to customers."  
William Blair 分析师 Dylan Becker 在一份报告中表示：“Samsara 在其不断增长的非基于车辆的产品套件中继续看到显着的势头，例如资产标签、设备监控、现场可见性、互联工作流程和培训。由于许多客户每年为丢失和被盗的资产分配八位数以上的资金，我们相信资产标签为 Samsara 提供了另一个机会，可以为客户提供实质性的有形价值。


Samsara's second-quarter earnings and revenue topped Wall Street targets amid strong growth in large accounts.  
在大客户强劲增长的情况下，Samsara 第二季度的收益和收入超过了华尔街的目标。


Samsara reported earnings of 5 cents per share on an adjusted basis versus a 1-cent profit a year earlier. Meanwhile, Q2 revenue rose 37% to $300 million, the company said.  
Samsara 报告称，调整后每股收益为 5 美分，而去年同期为 1 美分。与此同时，该公司表示，第二季度收入增长 37% 至 3 亿美元。


Analysts expected Samsara to record 1 cent profit on sales of $289.5 million.  
分析师预计 Samsara 将以 2.895 亿美元的销售额创下 1 美分的利润。


Further, annual recurring revenue from subscriptions rose 36% to $1.26 billion, topping estimates of $1.25 billion.  
此外，订阅的年度经常性收入增长 36% 至 12.6 亿美元，高于 12.5 亿美元的预期。


Samsara Stock Technical RatingsSamsara Stock 技术评级
-------------------------------------------------


Samsara stock holds an IBD Composite Rating of 97 out of a best-possible 99, according to [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-samsara-iot.aspx).  
根据 [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-samsara-iot.aspx) 的数据，Samsara 股票的 IBD 综合评级为 97 分（满分 99 分）。


IBD's Composite Rating combines five separate proprietary ratings into one easy-to-use rating. Also, the best growth stocks have a Composite Rating of 90 or better.  
IBD 的综合评级将五个独立的专有评级合并为一个易于使用的评级。此外，最好的成长型股票的综合评级为 90 或更高。


Further, Samsara stock has an Accumulation/Distribution Rating of B+. That rating analyzes price and volume changes in a stock over the past 13 weeks of trading. Its current rating indicates more funds are buying than selling.  
此外，Samsara 股票的累积/派发评级为 B+。该评级分析了过去 13 周交易期间股票的价格和成交量变化。其目前的评级表明，买入的基金多于卖出的基金。


The rating, on an A+ to E scale, measures institutional buying and selling in a stock. A+ signifies heavy institutional buying; E means heavy selling. Think of the C grade as neutral.  
该评级采用 A+ 到 E 的等级，衡量机构对股票的买卖。A+ 表示机构大量购买;E 表示重卖。将 C 级视为中性。
