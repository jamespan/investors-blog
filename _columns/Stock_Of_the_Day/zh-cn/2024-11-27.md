**Travelers Cos.** ([TRV](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TRV)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as it eyes a breakout to all-time highs amid an earnings boom. Rally For Dow Jones Stock道琼斯股票反弹
--------------------------------


Travelers, a Dow Jones stock, has rallied 17% so far this quarter on earnings and the postelection rally.  
道琼斯股票 Travelers 本季度迄今因收益和选后反弹而上涨了 17%。


A huge earnings beat on Oct. 17 led to a flurry of analyst price-target hikes, FactSet shows. But the average rating of 29 analysts covering Travelers stock is hold.  
FactSet 显示，10 月 17 日的巨额收益超出预期，导致分析师一连串的目标价上调。但 29 位分析师对 Travelers 股票的平均评分保持不变。




On Nov. 20, Janney Montgomery Scott analyst Robert Farnam joined that group, citing valuation concerns after a solid run for shares, according to TheFly.com.  
据 TheFly.com 称，11 月 20 日，Janney Montgomery Scott 分析师 Robert Farnam 加入了该小组，理由是在股价强劲上涨后出现估值担忧。


Founded in 1853, Travelers provides commercial insurance and personal insurance, as well as bond and specialty insurance.  
Travelers 成立于 1853 年，提供商业保险和个人保险，以及债券和专业保险。


Broadly, the insurance market is undergoing vast change. Insurers are shifting regional coverage and underwriting policies, exiting some markets while increasing premiums and reducing coverage in others. The result is creating winners and losers, with larger players tending to pick up market share.  


Industry peer **Gallagher** ([AJG](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AJG)), an insurance broker, is a [Long-Term Leader](https://www.investors.com/research/best-stocks-to-buy-now-long-term-stocks-ibd-long-term-leaders-list/). Insurance is a core **Berkshire Hathaway** ([BRKB](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BRKB)) business. But app-based insurance upstarts **Lemonade** ([LMND](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=LMND)) and **Root** ([ROOT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ROOT)) have far outperformed their established peers in the past year.  


Travelers Stock, Insurance Stocks
---------------------------------


Shares of Travelers rose 1.1% on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/research/stock-market-data-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-spdr-etfs/), above all the shorter- and longer-term moving averages. Travelers stock neared a 269.56 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/) from a flat base, according to [MarketSurge pattern recognition](https://marketsurge.investors.com). Shares traded just 1% below the entry on Wednesday.  



The [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/growth-stocks-breakout-specialty-tool-relative-strength-line/) for TRV stock is rising fast but remains below the peak of the base for now. It would send a positive signal to see the RS line move to new highs as the Dow Jones insurance company tries to break out. A rising RS line means that a stock is outperforming the S&P 500 index. It is the blue line in the chart provided.  


Travelers stock surged to all-time highs on earnings in October. It then shaped the six-week flat base, which formed entirely above the 50-day moving average, a favorable sign.  


On Wednesday, **Hartford Financial Services** ([HIG](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HIG)), an insurance stock, topped a 123.23 flat-base buy point. Class-B shares of Berkshire Hathaway stock cleared a 484.82 flat-base buy point intraday, but close below the entry.  


Travelers Earnings Boom
-----------------------


The [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-travelers-trv.aspx) tool shows that Travelers stock earns a solid [Composite Rating](https://education.investors.com/financialdictionary.aspx?mode=1&term=Composite+Rating+SmartSelect&termid=1) of 92 out of 99. The rating rolls various fundamental and technical metric into one easy-to-use score.  


In terms of other key ratings, Travelers shows an RS Rating of 83. It flaunts a superior EPS Rating of 96, also out of a best-possible 99.  


Earnings growth has averaged 1,422% over the past three quarters, vs. the more historically typical three-year rate of 2%. That reflects a huge earnings recovery amid higher underwriting volumes, higher interest rates and higher stock markets.  


For the third quarter, Travelers delivered a 169% leap in operating earnings per share to $5.24, despite higher catastrophe losses. That far outpaced estimates for $3.66 and benefited from easy year-ago comparisons.  


Travelers tied Q3 catastrophe losses mainly to Hurricane Helene.  


In Q3, net premiums written rose almost 8% to record levels. The combined ratio, a key profitability metric, improved by almost 8 points to 93.2%. Net investment income jumped 18%.  


Travelers also realized a net $42 million in after-tax investment gains. That contrasted with net realized investment losses in the year-ago quarter.  
Travelers 还实现了 4200 万美元的税后投资净收益。这与去年同期的已实现净投资亏损形成鲜明对比。


"Both underwriting income and underlying margins were strong in all three of our segments," Travelers Chief Executive Officer Alan Schnitzer said in a release.  
“我们所有三个细分市场的承保收入和基本利润率都很强劲，”Travelers 首席执行官 Alan Schnitzer 在一份新闻稿中表示。


He added: "With this momentum, we are very confident in our outlook for our business into 2025 and beyond."  
他补充说：“凭借这一势头，我们对 2025 年及以后的业务前景非常有信心。

For the full year, analysts project Travelers earnings per share will grow 44.3%. That would compare with a 5.7% rise last year. Analysts expect a further 12.1% gain in 2025.  
分析师预计全年 Travelers 的每股收益将增长 44.3%。相比之下，去年的涨幅为 5.7%。分析师预计 2025 年将进一步增长 12.1%。


After the Nov. 5 U.S. election, [financial stocks broadly led the stock market's Trump rally](https://www.investors.com/news/bank-stocks-break-out-broad-financial-rally-trump-win/). The result stoked optimism about looser regulations on financial companies.  
11 月 5 日美国大选后，[金融股广泛领跑股市的特朗普反弹](https://www.investors.com/news/bank-stocks-break-out-broad-financial-rally-trump-win/)。这一结果激起了人们对放宽金融公司监管的乐观情绪。

Year to date, Travelers stock has surged 40% on the back of two breakouts, while a third looms. It is the No. 7 top-performing Dow Jones stock in 2024 through Tuesday.  
年初至今，Travelers 的股票在两次突破后飙升了 40%，而第三次则迫在眉睫。截至周二，它是 2024 年道琼斯表现最好的第 7 只股票。
