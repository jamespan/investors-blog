**Apple** ([AAPL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AAPL)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the consumer electronics giant unveils a new iPad Mini tablet with artificial intelligence capabilities.  
**Apple** （[AAPL）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AAPL) 是[当今 IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为这家消费电子巨头推出了一款具有人工智能功能的新款 iPad Mini 平板电脑。


In morning trades on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/), Apple stock jumped 2% to 235.94. With the move, it broke out of a [handle](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/everything-you-need-to-know-about-handles-how-to-spot-a-good-one/) [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/the-best-stocks-have-crystal-clear-buy-points-heres-how-to-identify-them/) of 233.09, according to [IBD analysis](https://get.investors.com/ibd-live/).  
在[今天股市](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/)早盘交易中，苹果股价上涨 2% 至 235.94 点。根据 [IBD 的分析](https://get.investors.com/ibd-live/)，随着此举，它突破了 233.09 的[手柄](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/everything-you-need-to-know-about-handles-how-to-spot-a-good-one/)[买入点](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/the-best-stocks-have-crystal-clear-buy-points-heres-how-to-identify-them/)。




Further, Apple stock is approaching another buy point of 237.23 out of a 14-week [consolidation pattern](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/shopify-stock-consolidation-pattern-led-to-beautiful-gains/), according to [IBD MarketSurge](https://get.investors.com/marketsurge/?src=apa1bq) charts.  
此外，根据 [IBD MarketSurge](https://get.investors.com/marketsurge/?src=apa1bq) 图表，苹果股票在 237.23 周[的盘整模式](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/shopify-stock-consolidation-pattern-led-to-beautiful-gains/)中接近另一个买入点 14。


Earlier on Tuesday, Apple introduced a new model [iPad Mini built for Apple Intelligence](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2024/10/apple-introduces-powerful-new-ipad-mini-built-for-apple-intelligence/), the company's take on AI.  
周二早些时候，Apple 推出了[一款为 Apple Intelligence 打造的新型号 iPad Mini，Apple Intelligence](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2024/10/apple-introduces-powerful-new-ipad-mini-built-for-apple-intelligence/) 是该公司对 AI 的演绎。


The latest iPad Mini features Apple's A17 Pro chip, an 8.3-inch display and comes in four case finishes. It also supports the Apple Pencil Pro stylus.  
最新的 iPad Mini 配备 Apple 的 A17 Pro 芯片、8.3 英寸显示屏，并提供四种外壳饰面。它还支持 Apple Pencil Pro 手写笔。


The mini tablet starts at $499 for the Wi-Fi-only model with 128 gigabytes of storage. It will be available on Oct. 23, with preorders starting today.  
迷你平板电脑的纯 Wi-Fi 型号起价为 499 美元，存储空间为 128 GB。它将于 10 月 23 日上市，今天开始预购。


The next major catalyst for Apple stock could be the company's fiscal fourth-quarter report on Oct. 31.  
苹果股票的下一个主要催化剂可能是该公司 10 月 31 日的第四财季报告。


Apple Stock Gets Buy Ratings苹果股票获得买入评级
--------------------------------------


Also Tuesday, Morgan Stanley analyst Erik Woodring reiterated his overweight, or buy, rating on Apple stock with a price target of 273.  
同样在周二，摩根士丹利分析师 Erik Woodring 重申了他对苹果股票的增持或买入评级，目标价为 273。


In a client note, Woodring said data on iPhone 16 shipments and sales are "encouraging and in line with recent cycles."  
伍德林在一份客户报告中表示，iPhone 16 的出货量和销售数据“令人鼓舞，并与最近的周期一致”。


Further, data from research firm IDC points to stronger-than-expected iPhone shipments in the third quarter, he said.  
此外，研究公司 IDC 的数据表明，第三季度 iPhone 出货量强于预期，他说。


Plus, the expected launch of Apple Intelligence for the iPhone on Oct. 28 will be a positive development, he said. The first batch of features will include an improved Siri digital assistant, photo clean-up tools, audio transcriptions, email summaries and more, he said.  
此外，他表示，预计 10 月 28 日推出的 iPhone 版 Apple Intelligence 将是一个积极的发展。他说，第一批功能将包括改进的 Siri 数字助理、照片清理工具、音频转录、电子邮件摘要等。


On Sunday, Evercore ISI analyst Amit Daryanani added Apple stock to his firm's "[tactical outperform](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/apple-stock-aapl-added-to-tactical-outperform-list/)" list.  
周日，Evercore ISI 分析师 Amit Daryanani 将苹果股票添加到其公司的“[战术跑赢大盘](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/apple-stock-aapl-added-to-tactical-outperform-list/)”名单中。


Apple stock is on the [IBD Tech Leaders](https://www.investors.com/data-tables/ibd-tech-leaders-oct-14-2024/) list. It has an [IBD Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-invest-stocks-to-buy-composite-rating/) of 90 out of 99, according to [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-apple-aapl.aspx).  
Apple 股票在 [IBD 技术领导者](https://www.investors.com/data-tables/ibd-tech-leaders-oct-14-2024/)名单上。根据 [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-apple-aapl.aspx) 的数据，它的 [IBD 综合评分](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-invest-stocks-to-buy-composite-rating/)为 90 分（满分 99 分）。
