**Toast** ([TOST](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TOST)) is Friday's [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/), as the restaurant software provider broke out into a buy zone. Cleveland Research initiated TOST stock with a buy rating.  
**Toast** （[TOST](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TOST)） 是周五的 [IBD 今日股票](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为这家餐厅软件提供商突破了买入区。Cleveland Research 以买入评级启动 TOST 股票。


Toast has already surged more than 50% in 2024, but Cleveland Research's 33 price target suggests further upside.  
Toast 在 2024 年已经飙升了 50% 以上，但 Cleveland Research 的 33 目标价表明还有进一步上涨的空间。


Cleveland Research projected Friday that the Boston-based company could continue to see market share gains and outperform consensus estimates in the near- and medium-term outlook.  
克利夫兰研究公司周五预测，这家总部位于波士顿的公司可能会继续看到市场份额增加，并在近期和中期前景中超过市场普遍预期。




Toast's products range from point-of-sale hardware, kitchen displays, payment processing, supplier and invoice management to payroll, delivery management, menu consultation and marketing programs.  
Toast 的产品范围从销售点硬件、厨房展示、支付处理、供应商和发票管理到工资单、交付管理、菜单咨询和营销计划。


The note from Cleveland Research comes after Bank of America on Sept. 17 raised its TOST price target to 28 from 26. Bank of America maintained a neutral rating.  
Cleveland Research 的这份报告是在美国银行于 9 月 17 日将其 TOST 目标价从 26 上调至 28 之后发布的。美国银行维持中性评级。


Toast reported better-than-expected second-quarter earnings and revenue in August. Analysts forecast Q3 EPS popping 180% with sales increasing 25%. For the full year, analyst consensus has earnings growing 444% to 49 cents per share. Profit is expected to hit $1.18 per share in 2027, which would represent a 1,211% increase compared to 2023 levels.  
Toast 8 月份公布的第二季度收益和收入好于预期。分析师预测第三季度每股收益将增长 180%，销售额增长 25%。对于全年，分析师普遍认为收益增长 444% 至每股 49 美分。预计 2027 年利润将达到每股 1.18 美元，与 2023 年的水平相比增长 1,211%。


Toast's main competitors include Square-parent **Block** ([SQ](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=SQ)), **Fiserv**'s ([FIS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FIS)) Clover, **Shift4** ([FOUR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FOUR)), Lightspeed, TouchBistro and SpotOn.  
Toast 的主要竞争对手包括 Square-parent **Block** （[SQ](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=SQ)）、**Fiserv** （[FIS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FIS)） Clover、**Shift4** （[FOUR）、](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FOUR)Lightspeed、TouchBistro 和 SpotOn。

Co-founders Chris Comparator, Steve Fredette and Aman Narang worked at Endeca, a business intelligence software firm. Oracle acquired Endeca in 2011. Comparator stepped down as chief executive in September 2023, with Narang taking the helm.  
联合创始人 Chris Comparator、Steve Fredette 和 Aman Narang 曾在商业智能软件公司 Endeca 工作。Oracle 于 2011 年收购了 Endoca。Comparator 于 2023 年 9 月辞去首席执行官一职，由 Narang 掌舵。


Toast was founded in 2012, but the stock didn't go public until September 2021. The initial public offering raised $870 million. In 2020, the company rolled out Toast Capital, which provides loans to restaurants.  
Toast 成立于 2012 年，但直到 2021 年 9 月才上市。首次公开募股筹集了 8.7 亿美元。2020 年，该公司推出了 Toast Capital，为餐馆提供贷款。


Toast has also been active in making acquisitions. In 2019 Toast bought StratEx for $44 million. Chicago-based StratEx provides human resources and payroll software.  
Toast 还积极进行收购。2019 年，Toast 以 4400 万美元的价格收购了 StratEx。总部位于芝加哥的 StratEx 提供人力资源和薪资软件。


In 2021, Toast also bought XtraChef for $48 million. XtraChef sells supplier management software. Then in 2022, Toast acquired Sling, which has employee scheduling software that allows workers to change shifts and communicate with colleagues.  
2021 年，Toast 还以 4800 万美元的价格收购了 XtraChef。XtraChef 销售供应商管理软件。然后在 2022 年，Toast 收购了 Sling，该公司拥有员工调度软件，允许员工换班并与同事沟通。


Based in Boston, the company has U.S. offices in Chicago, Omaha, Reno, Nev.; San Francisco and Nashville. Toast's software runs on cloud-computing infrastructure at Amazon Web Services, part of **Amazon.com** ([AMZN](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AMZN)).  
该公司总部位于波士顿，在美国芝加哥、奥马哈、内华达州里诺;旧金山和纳什维尔。Toast 的软件在 Amazon Web Services 的云计算基础设施上运行，Amazon Web Services 是 **Amazon.com** [AMZN](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AMZN) 的一部分。


Toast Stock Performance Toast Stock 性能
--------------------------------------


TOST shares jumped 2.4% to 27.99 during [market trade](https://www.investors.com/category/market-trend/stock-market-today/) on Friday, hitting the best levels since early 2022 as they broke out above an official [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/).  
TOST 股价在周五[的市场交易](https://www.investors.com/category/market-trend/stock-market-today/)中上涨 2.4% 至 27.99，触及 2022 年初以来的最佳水平，突破官方[买入点](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/)。


The stock is exiting a consolidation going back to mid-July or mid-May, attempting to decisively clear a trading range going back to early 2023.  
该股正在退出可追溯到 7 月中旬或 5 月中旬的盘整，试图果断地清除可追溯到 2023 年初的交易区间。


Toast stock is now just above a 27.88 entry, according to [MarketSurge chart analysis](https://marketsurge.investors.com/).  
根据 [MarketSurge 图表分析](https://marketsurge.investors.com/)，Toast 股票现在略高于 27.88 的入场点。


Shares hit an intraday high of 29.92. Two prior breakouts quickly fizzled.  
股价盘中触及 29.92 点的高点。之前的两次突破很快就失败了。



The stock is up more than 12% in September after a 5% decline in August. To date in 2024, Toast stock has surged around 56%, outperforming the broader [IBD tracked Finance-Credit Card/Payments](https://marketsurge.investors.com/) industry group, which has rallied 10.5%.  
该股在 8 月份下跌 5% 后，9 月份上涨了 12% 以上。截至 2024 年，Toast 股票已飙升约 56%，跑赢了更广泛的 [IBD 跟踪金融信用卡/支付](https://marketsurge.investors.com/)行业组，后者上涨了 10.5%。


Meanwhile, Toast rival Shift4 stock traded higher in a buy zone Friday, 3% above an official 84.26 buy point.  
与此同时，Toast 的竞争对手 Shift4 股票周五在买入区走高，比官方的 84.26 买入点高出 3%。


Toast stock has a 70 [Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stocks-to-buy-and-watch-ibd-composite-rating-top-growth-stocks/) out of a best-possible 99. The stock also has an 86 [Relative Strength Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/) and a 54 [EPS Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/eps-rating-is-key-to-picking-great-stocks/).  
Toast stock 的综合[评分](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stocks-to-buy-and-watch-ibd-composite-rating-top-growth-stocks/)为 70 分（满分 99 分）。该股还拥有 86 的[相对强弱评级](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/)和 54 [的 EPS 评级](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/eps-rating-is-key-to-picking-great-stocks/)。
