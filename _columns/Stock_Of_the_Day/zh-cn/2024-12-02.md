**Apple** ([AAPL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AAPL)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as Wall Street focuses on iPhone 16 demand amid the holiday shopping season. In a positive for Apple stock, promotions among wireless firms heated up on Black Friday as **Verizon Communications** ([VZ](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=VZ)) sweetened its phone trade-in offer for customers.  
**苹果** （[AAPL）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AAPL) 是[当今的 IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为华尔街在假日购物季关注 iPhone 16 的需求。对苹果股票有利的是，随着 **Verizon Communications** （[VZ](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=VZ)） 为客户提供更甜蜜的手机以旧换新优惠，无线公司的促销活动在黑色星期五升温。


The big question for Apple stock continues to be whether "Apple Intelligence" – new artificial intelligence features — spurs demand for iPhone 16 models. In December, Apple is expected to start updating iPhones with its 18.2 iOS release, which includes AI features.  
苹果股票面临的最大问题仍然是“Apple Intelligence”——新的人工智能功能——是否会刺激对 iPhone 16 机型的需求。预计 Apple 将于 12 月开始更新其 18.2 iOS 版本，其中包括 AI 功能。


On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/?), Apple stock 1% to close at 239.59.  
[在今天的股市](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/?)上，苹果股价上涨 1%，收于 239.59 点。


Apple stock is breaking out of a flat base with a 237.49 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/apple-stock-set-up-proper-buy-point-before-big-rally/).  
苹果股票以 237.49 [的买入点](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/apple-stock-set-up-proper-buy-point-before-big-rally/)突破平坦的底部。




One bullish sign is that the flat base from which Apple is emerging is only 7% deep, compared with 17% for the prior 13-week consolidation.  
一个看涨的迹象是，苹果崛起的平坦底部仅为 7%，而前 13 周的盘整为 17%。


Apple Stock: Wireless Holiday PromotionsApple Stock： 无线假日促销
-----------------------------------------------------------


"Black Friday officially kicks off an important holiday season for Apple as its flagship iPhone 16 represents the beginning of its Apple Intelligence launch with the release of 18.2 iOS," Wedbush analyst Daniel Ives said in a note to clients.  
“黑色星期五正式拉开了苹果重要假期的序幕，因为其旗舰 iPhone 16 代表了其 Apple Intelligence 发布的开始，发布了 18.2 iOS，”Wedbush 分析师丹尼尔·艾夫斯 （Daniel Ives） 在给客户的一份报告中说。


He added: "We would characterize demand for iPhone 16 so far since its September launch as slightly ahead to generally in line with expectations globally."  
他补充说：“我们认为，自 9 月发布以来，对 iPhone 16 的需求略有领先，总体上符合全球预期。


UBS analyst David Vogt on Monday published a note based on a consumer survey. "The survey points to soft iPhone demand as AI interest remains muted," Vogt wrote.  
瑞银分析师大卫·沃格特 （David Vogt） 周一发布了一份基于消费者调查的说明。“调查显示，由于 AI 兴趣仍然低迷，iPhone 需求疲软，”Vogt 写道。


Craig Moffett, analyst at MoffettNathanson, in a report focused on promotions from wireless service providers – a key sales channel for Apple. Moffett noted that Verizon earlier this year in a trade-in offer began accepting "any smartphone, any condition" from customers rather than newer models.  
MoffettNathanson 的分析师 Craig Moffett 在一份报告中重点介绍了无线服务提供商的促销活动，这是 Apple 的关键销售渠道。Moffett 指出，Verizon 今年早些时候在以旧换新报价中开始接受客户的“任何智能手机、任何条件”，而不是更新型号。


"This year's newest Black Friday offers — some introduced just in the past few days — go a step further," Moffett said. "Led once again by Verizon, the whole market has started offering promotional discounts with no trade-in required."  
“今年最新的黑色星期五优惠——有些是在过去几天才推出的——更进一步，”莫菲特说。“在 Verizon 的再次引领下，整个市场已经开始提供无需以旧换新的折扣。”


Still, the impact on iPhone 16 demand remains to be seen.  
尽管如此，对 iPhone 16 需求的影响仍有待观察。


Earnings Top Estimates 收益最高估计
-----------------------------


"Generally speaking, more (promotions) — especially with retention offers for existing subscribers — would obviously be good for Apple," Moffett said.  
“一般来说，更多的（促销活动）——尤其是为现有用户提供保留优惠——显然对苹果有好处，”莫菲特说。


With Monday's gain, Apple stock has advanced about 24% in 2024.  
随着周一的上涨，苹果股票在 2024 年上涨了约 24%。


Apple reported [fiscal fourth-quarter adjusted earnings](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/apple-stock-aapl-fiscal-q4-2024-earnings/) of $1.64 a share on sales of $94.93 billion in the quarter ended Sept. 28. Analysts polled by FactSet had expected earnings of $1.60 a share on sales of $94.5 billion.  
Apple 报告称，截至 9 月 28 日的季度[，第四财季调整后每股收益](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/apple-stock-aapl-fiscal-q4-2024-earnings/)为 1.64 美元，销售额为 949.3 亿美元。接受 FactSet 调查的分析师此前预计每股收益为 1.60 美元，销售额为 945 亿美元。


On a year-over-year basis, Apple earnings rose 12% while sales increased 6%.  
与去年同期相比，苹果收益增长 12%，销售额增长 6%。


Apple Stock Technical RatingsApple Stock 技术评级
---------------------------------------------


Apple stock is on the IBD Tech Leaders list. It holds a Composite Rating of 91 from Investor's Business Daily, according to [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-apple-aapl.aspx).  
Apple 股票在 IBD 技术领导者名单上。根据 [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-apple-aapl.aspx) 的数据，它拥有 Investor's Business Daily 的 91 综合评级。


IBD's [Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-research-growth-stocks/) combines five separate proprietary ratings into one easy-to-use rating. The best growth stocks have a Composite Rating of 90 or better.  
IBD [的综合评级](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-research-growth-stocks/)将五个独立的专有评级合并为一个易于使用的评级。最好的成长型股票的综合评级为 90 或更高。


Further, Apple stock has an Accumulation/Distribution Rating of C. That rating analyzes price and volume changes in a stock over the past 13 weeks of trading. Its current rating indicates more funds are buying than selling.  
此外，Apple 股票的累积/派发评级为 C。该评级分析了过去 13 周交易期间股票的价格和成交量变化。其目前的评级表明，买入的基金多于卖出的基金。


The rating, on a scale of A+ to E, measures institutional buying and selling in a stock. A+ signifies heavy institutional buying; E means heavy selling. Think of the C grade as neutral.  
该评级采用 A+ 到 E 的等级，衡量机构对股票的买卖。A+ 表示机构大量购买;E 表示重卖。将 C 级视为中性。
