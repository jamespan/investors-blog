**Deckers Brands** ([DECK](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=DECK)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) for Thursday as shares of the Hoka maker test a buy point. Footwear stocks swung generally higher as **Under Armor** ([UAA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UAA)) rallied 31% on earnings.  
**Deckers Brands** （[DECK）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=DECK) 是周四的 [IBD 每日股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为 Hoka 制造商的股票测试了一个买入点。鞋类股普遍走高，**Under Armor** （[UAA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UAA)） 因收益上涨 31%。


Deckers, the seller behind the popular Hoka and Ugg shoe brands, has received praise from analysts as it rides momentum into the holiday shopping season. The company on Oct. 24 reported a 39% increase for Q2 2025 earnings to $1.59 per share, beating FactSet estimates of $1.24 per share. Revenue jumped 20% to $1.31 billion, while analysts expected $1.2 billion.  
Deckers 是广受欢迎的 Hoka 和 Ugg 鞋履品牌背后的销售商，随着它乘势进入假日购物季，它受到了分析师的赞扬。该公司于 10 月 24 日报告称，2025 年第二季度的收益增长 39% 至每股 1.59 美元，高于 FactSet 估计的每股 1.24 美元。收入增长 20% 至 13.1 亿美元，而分析师预期为 12 亿美元。




Hoka brand sales soared nearly 35% to $570.9 million. UGG sales rose 13% to $690 million, while Teva brand sales ticked up 2.3%. Sanuk net sales tumbled almost 48% to $2.8 million. Deckers completed the sale of its Sanuk brand on Aug. 15 to athleticwear company Lolë for an undisclosed sum.  
Hoka 品牌销售额飙升近 35% 至 5.709 亿美元。UGG 销售额增长 13% 至 6.9 亿美元，而 Teva 品牌销售额增长 2.3%。Sanuk 的净销售额下降了近 48% 至 280 万美元。Deckers 于 8 月 15 日完成了将其 Sanuk 品牌出售给运动服公司 Lolë 的交易，收购金额未披露。


Deckers guided to a 12% increase in 2025 sales, to $4.8 billion. The company forecasts earnings ranging from $5.15 to $5.25 per share, up from $4.86 per share last year. The consensus view among analysts polled by FactSet calls for 2025 earnings of $5.36 per share on $4.83 billion in sales.  
Deckers 预计 2025 年的销售额增长 12%，达到 48 亿美元。该公司预计收益为每股 5.15 美元至 5.25 美元，高于去年的每股 4.86 美元。FactSet 调查的分析师一致认为，2025 年的销售额为 48.3 亿美元，每股收益为 5.36 美元。


For Deckers' third quarter, FactSet expects earnings to slide 2% to $2.52 per share. Analysts predict a 9% increase in revenue, to $1.7 billion.  
对于 Deckers 的第三季度，FactSet 预计收益将下滑 2% 至每股 2.52 美元。分析师预测收入将增长 9%，达到 17 亿美元。


Resounding Beat, Strong Runway响亮的节拍，强劲的跑道
-----------------------------------------


KeyBanc called the report a "resounding beat on all fronts," noting upside potential based on Deckers' new product launches and "strong international runway." The firm raised its price target on DECK stock to 190 from 180 and maintained an overweight rating on the shares.  
KeyBanc 称该报告“在各个方面都取得了响亮的成绩”，并指出基于 Deckers 新产品发布和“强大的国际跑道”的上行潜力。该公司将 DECK 股票的目标价从 180 上调至 190，并维持对该股的增持评级。


Telsey Advisory lifted its price target on Deckers stock following its results, noting the company outperformed expectations across the board. Sales growth nearly doubled market expectations, led by strong gains at Hoka and Ugg, the firm added. Deckers continues to deliver results in an uncertain macro environment, which demonstrates its strong market position, healthy brand portfolio and ability to drive long-term growth, Telsey wrote. The firm lifted its price target on DECK stock to 190 from 183 on the results and kept an outperform rating. Telsey in late December also added the Hoka maker to its top picks for the 2024 holiday season among footwear stocks, along with **Birkenstock** ([BIRK](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BIRK)).  
Telsey Advisory 在业绩公布后上调了 Deckers 股票的目标价，并指出该公司的整体表现均超出预期。该公司补充说，销售额增长几乎是市场预期的两倍，其中 Hoka 和 Ugg 的强劲增长引领了这一增长。Telsey 写道，Deckers 在不确定的宏观环境中继续取得成果，这表明其强大的市场地位、健康的品牌组合和推动长期增长的能力。该公司将 DECK 股票的目标价从 183 上调至 190，并保持跑赢大盘评级。Telsey 在 12 月下旬还将 Hoka 制造商与 **Birkenstock** （[BIRK）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BIRK) 一起列入 2024 年假日季鞋类股票的首选。

Shares of the Hoka maker have rallied more than 28% from an August low and are up almost 55% in 2024.  
这家 Hoka 制造商的股价从 8 月份的低点上涨了 28% 以上，并在 2024 年上涨了近 55%。


Deckers hit a record high of 184.48 on June 3 before sliding into a consolidation.  
Deckers 在 6 月 3 日创下 184.48 的历史新高，然后滑入盘整。


Under Armor vaulted more than 31% after its quarterly results, propelling shoe stocks higher. Under Armor posted a 58% increase in earnings to 30 cents per share, outpacing estimates for 20 cents. Revenue fell almost 11% to $1.399 billion, but still edged out expectations for $1.386 billion. The company also lifted its earnings guidance ahead of forecasts as it makes progress on its restructuring plan.  
Under Armor 在季度业绩公布后上涨超过 31%，推动鞋类股走高。Under Armor 公布的收益增长 58% 至每股 30 美分，高于 20 美分的预期。收入下降近 11% 至 13.99 亿美元，但仍略高于预期的 13.86 亿美元。该公司还提前了盈利指引，因为其重组计划取得了进展。


Among other footwear stocks, **On Holding** ([ONON](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ONON)) popped 5% and bounced off its [50-day moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-50-day-moving-average-when-to-buy-or-sell-growth-stocks/).  
在其他鞋类股中，**On Holding** （[ONON）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ONON) 上涨 5%，并从 [50 天移动平均线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-50-day-moving-average-when-to-buy-or-sell-growth-stocks/)反弹。


On Holding reports its Q3 results on Nov. 12. FactSet expects flat earnings year over year at 22 cents per share on nearly 31% revenue growth to $705 million.  
On Holding 于 11 月 12 日报告了其第三季度业绩。FactSet 预计每股收益为 22 美分，收入增长近 31% 至 7.05 亿美元。


**Crocs** ([CROX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CROX)) climbed 1.2% while Birkenstock rose 2.2%.  
**卡骆驰** （[CROX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CROX)） 上涨 1.2%，而勃肯上涨 2.2%。
