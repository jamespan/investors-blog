Chubb, an insurance favorite of Warren Buffett, is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) for Thursday. **Chubb** ([CB](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CB)) shares broke out above a buy point as insurance stocks rallied on **Travelers** ([TRV](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TRV)) earnings.  
沃伦·巴菲特 （Warren Buffett） 最喜欢的保险业 Chubb 是周四的 [IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)。**安达** （[CB）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CB) 股价突破买入点，因为保险股因 **Travelers** （[TRV](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TRV)） 的收益而上涨。


Early Thursday, Dow Jones component [Travelers reported a 169% increase in earnings](https://www.investors.com/news/travelers-earnings-dow-jones-trv-stock-breakout-mmc-stock-insurance/) to $5.24 per share, easily beating FactSet estimates of $3.66 per share. Total revenue jumped 12% to $11.9 billion, outpacing expectations for $11.69 billion.  
周四早些时候，道琼斯成分股 [Travelers 报告称，收益增长 169%](https://www.investors.com/news/travelers-earnings-dow-jones-trv-stock-breakout-mmc-stock-insurance/) 至每股 5.24 美元，轻松超过了 FactSet 估计的每股 3.66 美元。总收入增长 12% 至 119 亿美元，高于预期的 116.9 亿美元。




Net written premiums increased 8% to a record $11.32 billion, but were shy of views for $11.38 billion. Travelers noted that net written premiums for business insurance rose 9%, while personal insurance and bond and specialty insurance premiums both rose 7% for the quarter.  
净承保保费增长 8% 至创纪录的 113.2 亿美元，但低于 113.8 亿美元的观点。旅行者指出，商业保险的净承保保费增长了 9%，而个人保险以及债券和特殊保险保费在本季度均增长了 7%。


Travelers' catastrophe losses increased 10.5% to $939 million, driven by Hurricane Helene and "severe wind and hail storms in multiple states."  
在飓风海伦和“多个州的强风和冰雹风暴”的推动下，旅行者的灾难损失增加了 10.5%，达到 9.39 亿美元。


However, analysts anticipated an increase in storm losses. BofA on Oct. 10 noted that Q3 was an active quarter for global catastrophe events, but said it expects catastrophe losses to be "manageable" for property and casualty insurers, The Fly reported. The firm sees Hurricane Milton generating some losses in Q4 on the back of Hurricane Helene, although uncertainty remains about the extent of the damages. In its insurance preview, BofA lifted its price target on Chubb stock to 282 from 275 but kept an underperform rating on the shares.  
然而，分析师预计风暴损失会增加。据 The Fly 报道，美国银行 10 月 10 日指出，第三季度是全球巨灾事件活跃的季度，但表示预计财产和意外伤害保险公司的巨灾损失将是“可控的”。该公司预计，在飓风“海伦”之后，飓风“米尔顿”将在第四季度造成一些损失，尽管损失程度仍存在不确定性。在其保险预览中，美国银行将安达股票的目标价从 275 上调至 282，但维持对该股的跑输大盘评级。


For Chubb's Q3 results on Oct. 30, FactSet expects earnings to tick up to $4.98 per share from $4.95 the year prior. Analysts forecast revenue jumps 7.6% to $14.16 billion. Chubb's earning growth slowed over the last two quarters, to a 9% gain in Q2 from a 108% gain in Q4. The insurer averaged 16% revenue growth during that period.  
对于 10 月 30 日安达的第三季度业绩，FactSet 预计每股收益将从去年的 4.95 美元上升至每股 4.98 美元。分析师预测收入增长 7.6% 至 141.6 亿美元。安达的盈利增长在过去两个季度有所放缓，从第四季度的 108% 增长到第二季度的 9%。在此期间，该保险公司的平均收入增长了 16%。


Warren Buffett, Chummy With Chubb沃伦·巴菲特（Warren Buffett），《与安达的亲密关系》（Chummy with Chubb）
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Meanwhile, investment oracle Warren Buffett has added to his sizable stake in Chubb stock after taking out a position earlier this year. Buffett's **Berkshire Hathaway** ([BRKB](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BRKB)) owns a 6.34% stake in Chubb, worth nearly $6.9 billion with just over 27 million shares. Buffett's firm increased its CB stock holdings to 2.56% of its total portfolio, up from 2.03% according to the latest regulatory filings tracked by [whalewisdom.com](https://whalewisdom.com/filer/berkshire-hathaway-inc). Buffett [first opened a $6.7 billion position in Chubb](https://www.investors.com/news/chubb-stock-breakout-warren-buffett-returns-to-insurance/) in mid-May, which prompted a breakout.  
与此同时，投资预言家沃伦·巴菲特 （Warren Buffett） 在今年早些时候建仓后，增加了他在 Chubb 股票中的大量股份。巴菲特的**伯克希尔哈撒韦**公司 （[BRKB）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BRKB) 拥有 Chubb 6.34% 的股份，价值近 69 亿美元，持有 2700 多万股。根据 whalewisdom.com 跟踪的最新监管文件，巴菲特的公司将其持有的 CB 股票占其总投资组合的 2.56% 从2.03%增加到2.56[%。](https://whalewisdom.com/filer/berkshire-hathaway-inc)巴菲特于 5 月中[旬首次在 Chubb 开设了 67 亿美元的头寸](https://www.investors.com/news/chubb-stock-breakout-warren-buffett-returns-to-insurance/)，这促使了突破。


**Apple** ([AAPL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AAPL)), **American Express** ([AXP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AXP)) and **Bank of America** ([BAC](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BAC)) are still Buffett's largest holdings, though Berkshire has slashed its Apple and BofA holdings in recent months. AAPL stock represents 31.2% of Berkshire Hathaway's total portfolio, AXP represents 13% and BAC accounts for about 11.5%.  
**苹果** （[AAPL）、](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AAPL)**美国运通** （[AXP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AXP)） 和美国**银行** （[BAC](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BAC)） 仍然是巴菲特最大的持股，尽管伯克希尔最近几个月大幅削减了其对苹果和美国银行的持股。AAPL 股票占伯克希尔哈撒韦公司总投资组合的 31.2%，AXP 占 13%，BAC 约占 11.5%。


Chubb Stock 安达股票
----------------



Chubb stock swung 2.4% higher Thursday to clear a 294.18 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/apple-stock-set-up-proper-buy-point-before-big-rally/) for a 10-week [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-a-flat-base-skechers-stock-skx/).  
安达股价周四上涨 2.4%，以 294.18 的[买入点](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/apple-stock-set-up-proper-buy-point-before-big-rally/)成交 10 周[flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-a-flat-base-skechers-stock-skx/)。


The [buy zone](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/buy-zone-nvidia-stock/), which stretches 5% beyond the buy point, extends to 308.88.  
[买入区](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/buy-zone-nvidia-stock/)超出买入点 5%，延伸至 308.88。


On Wednesday, CB stock climbed 2%, rallying off its [21-day exponential moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/) on Oct. 11  
周三，CB 股价上涨 2%，从 10 月 11 日的 [21 天指数移动平均线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/)反弹


CB stock traded tightly for the five weeks leading into the start of October. Shares tumbled below its [50-day line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-50-day-moving-average-when-to-buy-or-sell-growth-stocks/) on Oct. 7 as Hurricane Milton fears hit insurers briefly. But Chubb rebounded above that key level two days later.  
CB 股票在截至 10 月初的五周内交易紧张。10 月 7 日，由于对飓风米尔顿的担忧短暂打击了保险公司，股价跌破 [50 日线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-50-day-moving-average-when-to-buy-or-sell-growth-stocks/)。但两天后，Chubb 反弹至该关键水平以上。


Still, Chubb stock has rallied more than 32% this year and is trading at record highs.  
尽管如此，安达的股票今年还是上涨了 32% 以上，交易价格创下历史新高。


TRV stock leapt 7.7% Thursday to lead the Dow Jones Industrial Average and gap out above a 243.48 buy point for a high [handle](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-buy-stocks-lam-research-cup-with-handle-launched-75-percent-advance/) or five-weeks-tight pattern next to a 21-week consolidation.  
TRV 股价周四上涨 7.7%，领跑道琼斯工业平均指数，并跳空突破 243.48 买入点，形成高[位](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-buy-stocks-lam-research-cup-with-handle-launched-75-percent-advance/)或 5 周紧身形态，接下来是 21 周的盘整。
