**SPX Technologies** ([SPXC](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=SPXC)) is Wednesday's [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the heating, ventilation and air conditioning, or HVAC, innovator capitalizes on growth opportunities serving data centers, drugmakers and more. SPXC stock edged higher on Thursday. It's one to keep on your watchlist as it etches out a handle at the end of a 10-week consolidation.  
**SPX Technologies** （[SPXC）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=SPXC) 是周三的 [IBD 今日 IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为供暖、通风和空调 （HVAC） 创新者利用服务于数据中心、制药商等的增长机会。SPXC 股价周四小幅走高。这是一个值得保留在您的观察列表中的股票，因为它会在 10 周的整合结束时刻出一个手柄。


SPX, based in Charlotte, N.C., has a stellar 98 IBD Composite Rating, according to [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/). The Composite Rating, on a 1-99 scale, combines both technical and fundamental factors in a single rating.  
根据 [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/) 的数据，总部位于北卡罗来纳州夏洛特的 SPX 拥有 98 个 IBD 综合评级。综合评级采用 1-99 分制，将技术和基本因素结合在一个评级中。




Data Centers Lift HVAC Sales数据中心电梯 HVAC 销售
------------------------------------------


"We continue to see strong demand for our cooling products across a broad set of end market applications, including data centers, health care facilities, semiconductor plants and industrial facilities," CEO Gene Lowe told analysts on the Aug. 1 second-quarter earnings call.  
“我们继续看到广泛的终端市场应用对我们的冷却产品的强劲需求，包括数据中心、医疗保健设施、半导体工厂和工业设施，”首席执行官 Gene Lowe 在 8 月 1 日的第二季度财报电话会议上告诉分析师。


HVAC makes up about 70% of SPX sales, with its Detection & Measurement business making up the rest  
HVAC约占SPX销售额的70%，其余部分由其检测和测量业务承担。


HVAC segment revenue grew 32.5% from a year ago, including 17.7% organic growth, according to a note from William Blair analyst Ross Sparenblek.  
根据 William Blair 分析师 Ross Sparenblek 的一份报告，HVAC 部门的收入比一年前增长了 32.5%，其中包括 17.7% 的有机增长。


The segment also saw a 15% boost from acquisitions of Ingenia and Aspeq that SPX has said expand its product line and its technological leadership.  
该细分市场还因收购 Ingenia 和 Aspeq 而增长了 15%，SPX 表示这扩大了其产品线和技术领先地位。


EPS has grown 34% in each of the past two quarters. Revenue growth has accelerated from 9% to 16% to 18% the past three quarters, amid acquisitions and a couple of large jobs that were completed in Q2.  
过去两个季度的每股收益均增长了 34%。过去三个季度，收入增长从 9% 加速到 16% 再到 18%，其中包括第二季度完成的收购和几个大型工作。


SPX Growth Outlook SPX 增长展望
---------------------------


The outlook provided by SPX on Aug. 1 implies "sequentially more moderate growth and margin expansion in the second half of 2024," Blair's Sparenblek wrote.  
Blair 的 Sparenblek 写道，SPX 在 8 月 1 日提供的展望意味着“2024 年下半年的增长和利润率将环比温和”。


William Blair rates SPXC stock as outperform. It sees a "fair value" at $160 per share, or 25 times earnings of $6.35 in 2025.  
William Blair 将 SPXC 股票评为跑赢大盘。它认为“公允价值”为每股 160 美元，或 2025 年收益 6.35 美元的 25 倍。


The firm sees a path to continued double-digit earnings growth as it consolidates fragmented markets and keeps deploying its ample capital for bolt-on acquisitions.  
该公司看到了一条持续两位数盈利增长的道路，因为它整合了分散的市场，并不断部署其充足的资本进行补强收购。


Blair also highlighted traction on new products, including its hybrid wet/dry Adiabatic Cooling Tower, and the ramping of production for various in-demand product lines, including the Everest Cooling Tower for data centers.  
Blair 还强调了新产品的吸引力，包括其混合湿/干绝热冷却塔，以及各种需求产品线的产量增加，包括用于数据中心的 Everest 冷却塔。


New construction of data centers, chip and EV plants, and other nonresidential products has boosted SPX's backlog. But the company derives about two-thirds of revenue from replacement sales.  
数据中心、芯片和电动汽车工厂以及其他非住宅产品的新建设增加了 SPX 的积压工作。但该公司大约三分之二的收入来自替换销售。


SPXC Stock SPXC 股票
------------------


SPX rose 0.8% to 159.76 in Wednesday [stock market action](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/), finding support just above the 21-day line once again. That puts SPX 3% below an official 164.91 buy point from a 10-week consolidation, according to [MarketSurge](https://marketsurge.investors.com/).  
SPX 在周三[的股市走势](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/)中上涨 0.8% 至 159.76，再次在 21 日线上方获得支撑。根据 [MarketSurge](https://marketsurge.investors.com/) 的数据，这使 SPX 在为期 10 周的盘整中比官方的 164.91 买入点低 3%。


SPX has been consolidating since mid-July, briefly cresting the [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) on Sept. 20, when SPXC touched an intraday high of 165.13.  
SPX 自 7 月中旬以来一直在盘整，在 9 月 20 日短暂达到[买入点](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/)的峰值，当时 SPXC 触及盘中高点 165.13。


Investors can treat that Sept. 20 peak as a high handle on the end of the consolidation. A break of the handle's downtrend with a convincing move higher would flash an early entry opportunity. The trigger could be the 10-day line, currently just above 160.  
投资者可以将 9 月 20 日的峰值视为盘整结束的高杠杆。突破手柄的下降趋势并令人信服地走高将预示着早期入场机会。触发因素可能是 10 日线，目前略高于 160。


SPXC is up roughly 58% year to date, as several HVAC companies have become AI-adjacent cooling plays.  
SPXC 今年迄今上涨了约 58%，因为几家 HVAC 公司已成为与 AI 相邻的制冷公司。
