**General Motors** ([GM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=GM)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) after delivering its third earnings beat-and-raise report this year. GM stock broke out powerfully to its highest level since February 2022.  
**通用汽车** （[GM）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=GM) 在发布今年第三份收益超过预期和上调报告后成为 [IBD 今日股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)。通用汽车股票强劲突破至 2022 年 2 月以来的最高水平。


In Q3, GM earnings surged 30% vs. a year earlier and revenue grew 11%, led by pickup truck demand in North America. [Earnings smashed Wall Street expectations](https://www.investors.com/news/gm-earnings-q3-gm-stock-tesla-ferrari-ford/) and revenue also beat. On Tuesday, the automaker again hiked its 2024 outlook, while highlighting Q3 achievements amid industry challenges.  
第三季度，通用汽车的收益与去年同期相比增长了 30%，收入增长了 11%，这得益于北美的皮卡需求。[收益打破了华尔街的预期](https://www.investors.com/news/gm-earnings-q3-gm-stock-tesla-ferrari-ford/)，收入也超过了华尔街。周二，这家汽车制造商再次上调了 2024 年的展望，同时强调了在行业挑战中取得的第三季度成就。




Auto Giant Grows Market Share But Issues Warning汽车巨头市场份额增加，但发出警告
----------------------------------------------------------------


"We grew U.S. retail market share with above-average pricing, well-managed inventories and below-average incentives," GM CEO Mary Barra said in a letter to shareholders. In other words, GM is growing share while spending less than other companies to incentivize car sales.  
“我们以高于平均水平的定价、管理良好的库存和低于平均水平的激励措施扩大了美国零售市场份额，”通用汽车首席执行官玛丽·巴拉 （Mary Barra） 在致股东的一封信中表示。换句话说，通用汽车的份额正在增长，而激励汽车销售的支出却比其他公司少。


Industry conditions remain challenging. On Tuesday, Barra cautioned investors against "mistaking progress for winning."  
行业状况仍然充满挑战。周二，巴拉警告投资者不要“将进步误认为是胜利”。


"Competition is fierce, and the regulatory environment will keep getting tougher," she wrote in a letter to shareholders. "That's why we are focused on optimizing our ICE (internal combustion engine) margins and working to make our EVs (electric vehicles) profitable on an EBIT (earnings before interest and taxes) basis as quickly as possible."  
“竞争很激烈，监管环境将越来越严峻，”她在给股东的一封信中写道。“这就是为什么我们专注于优化我们的 ICE（内燃机）利润率，并努力使我们的 EV（电动汽车）尽快在 EBIT（息税前利润）的基础上实现盈利。”


The statement seemed to allude to both emissions regulations and the 2024 U.S. election, with presidential candidates Donald Trump and Kamala Harris jousting on key EV, tax and tariff policies.  
该声明似乎暗示了排放法规和 2024 年美国大选，总统候选人唐纳德·特朗普和卡马拉·哈里斯就关键的电动汽车、税收和关税政策进行了角逐。


GM Stock Gaps Up On Q3 Earnings Beat-And-Raise通用汽车股票在第三季度收益跑赢上调后跳空上涨
--------------------------------------------------------------------


Shares of General Motors soared 9.8% in huge volume on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/research/stock-market-data-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-spdr-etfs/) after the Q3 report. GM stock rocketed out of a first-stage cup-with-handle base with a 49.86 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/) on Tuesday, according to [MarketSurge pattern recognition](https://marketsurge.investors.com), while hitting its best level since February 2022. GM stock is now extended from the buy zone.  
第三季度报告发布后，通用汽车的股价[今天在股市](https://www.investors.com/research/stock-market-data-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-spdr-etfs/)上飙升了 9.8%。根据 [MarketSurge 模式识别](https://marketsurge.investors.com)，通用汽车股票周二以 49.86 [的买入点](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/)从第一阶段带把手杯的基础上飙升，同时触及 2022 年 2 月以来的最佳水平。通用汽车股票现在从买入区扩展。



The [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/growth-stocks-breakout-specialty-tool-relative-strength-line/) hit a fresh 2024 high as the emerging EV stock broke out, a positive sign. That is marked by a blue dot at the end of the RS line on the weekly MarketSurge chart.  
随着新兴电动汽车股票的爆发，[相对强弱线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/growth-stocks-breakout-specialty-tool-relative-strength-line/)创下 2024 年新高，这是一个积极的信号。这在每周 MarketSurge 图表的 RS 线末端由一个蓝点标记。


Earlier in October, GM stock bounced off support at the 50-day moving average. During an investor event on Oct. 8, General Motors said that its electric cars will soon cross over to profitability.  
10 月初，通用汽车股票从 50 天移动平均线的支撑位反弹。在 10 月 8 日的投资者活动中，通用汽车表示，其电动汽车将很快实现盈利。


Growing EV Rival To Tesla And Ford日益增长的电动汽车竞争对手特斯拉和福特
-----------------------------------------------------


Few companies make money on electric vehicles right now besides Tesla and a few of its Chinese competitors. Unlike Ford, General Motors does not break down EV losses. On Tuesday, GM merely indicated that rising EV volumes hurt third-quarter results.  
除了特斯拉及其一些中国竞争对手之外，目前很少有公司通过电动汽车赚钱。与福特不同，通用汽车不细分电动汽车的损失。周二，通用汽车仅表示，电动汽车销量的增长损害了第三季度的业绩。


In Q3, GM became the No. 2 EV seller in the U.S. behind **Tesla** ([TSLA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSLA)), knocking **Ford** ([F](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=F)) off that perch.  
第三季度，通用汽车成为美国第二大电动汽车销售商，仅次于**特斯拉** （[TSLA），](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSLA)将**福特** （[F](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=F)） 淘汰出局。


Tesla reports Q3 earnings on Wednesday and Ford will follow on Oct. 28.  
特斯拉将于周三公布第三季度财报，福特将于 10 月 28 日公布财报。


Strong Demand, Share Buybacks Fuel GM Earnings Outlook强劲的需求和股票回购推动了通用汽车的盈利前景
----------------------------------------------------------------------------


This year, GM stock is far outpacing shares of Ford and Tesla on the back of a series of guidance hikes and billions of dollars in share buybacks.  
今年，通用汽车的股价远超福特和特斯拉的股价，这得益于一系列指引性上调和数十亿美元的股票回购。


That performance flies in the face of industry woes, including inflation, high interest rates and Chinese competition.  
这一表现与行业困境相悖，包括通货膨胀、高利率和中国的竞争。


For 2024, GM is now guiding EPS of $10-$10.50, with the $10.25 midpoint above prior guidance of $9.50-$10.50. Going into the report, analysts expected 2024 EPS of $9.97, a 30% increase from 2023.  
对于 2024 年，通用汽车现在的每股收益指导为 10-10.50 美元，10.25 美元的中点比之前的 9.50-10.50 美元指导高出。在报告中，分析师预计 2024 年的每股收益为 9.97 美元，比 2023 年增长 30%。


Year to date, GM stock has now soared more than 49% after Tuesday's earnings breakaway gap. Still, it remains not that much higher than where it was a decade ago.  
年初至今，通用汽车的股票在周二的收益突破缺口后飙升了 49% 以上。尽管如此，它仍然没有比十年前高多少。
