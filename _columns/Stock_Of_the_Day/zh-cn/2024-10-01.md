**Ryan Specialty** ([RYAN](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RYAN)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as it tries to break out to fresh highs. RYAN stock briefly offered an early entry as it broke a downtrend from a September interim high.  
**Ryan Specialty** （[RYAN）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RYAN) 是[当今的 IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)，因为它试图突破新高。RYAN 股票短暂提供早期入场，因为它打破了 9 月中期高点的下跌趋势。


The provider of specialty products and solutions for insurance brokers and agents boasts robust, double-digit earnings and revenue growth. A leading excess and surplus (E&S) insurance company, Ryan has expanded through strategic acquisitions and organic growth.  
这家为保险经纪人和代理人提供专业产品和解决方案的供应商拥有强劲的两位数收入和收入增长。Ryan 是一家领先的超额和盈余 （E&S） 保险公司，通过战略收购和有机增长进行扩张。


Financial and insurance stocks tend to benefit as interest rates rise. But they can also benefit from falling rates. As the Fed starts to cut rates, lending and refinancing may perk up. These companies may also be able to borrow more money at lower rates.  
随着利率上升，金融和保险股往往会受益。但他们也可以从利率下降中受益。随着美联储开始降息，贷款和再融资可能会增加。这些公司也可能能够以较低的利率借到更多的钱。




RYAN Stock Flirts With Buy PointRYAN 股票与 Buy Point 调情
-----------------------------------------------------


Shares popped 2.7% to 68.20 on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/research/stock-market-data-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-spdr-etfs/) in above-average volume. RYAN stock briefly topped a 69.03 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/) from a cup-shaped base on Tuesday. The insurance stock is trading just above an early, not-quite-handle entry of 68.47, which marks the Sept. 24 interim high.  
今天，股价在[股市](https://www.investors.com/research/stock-market-data-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-spdr-etfs/)上上涨 2.7% 至 68.20，成交量高于平均水平。RYAN 股票周二从杯形底部短暂突破 69.03 [买入点](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/)。保险股的交易价格略高于早盘的未完全成交价 68.47，这标志着 9 月 24 日的中期高点。


Aggressive traders could have bought Ryan Specialty as it broke the downtrend of the quasi-handle.  
激进的交易者可能会购买 Ryan Specialty，因为它打破了准手柄的下降趋势。


Shares found support at the 10-week moving average in late September.  
股价在 9 月下旬的 10 周移动平均线找到支撑。


Ryan Specialty insurance stock remains above all the short- and long-term support levels, including the [21-day exponential moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/). It has been riding those levels since the cup base began to form nine weeks ago.  
Ryan Specialty 保险股仍然高于所有短期和长期支撑位，包括 [21 天指数移动平均线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/)。自从九周前杯赛基础开始形成以来，它一直在这些水平上徘徊。



The [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-line-gives-crucial-clues-about-stocks-ready-to-make-big-gains/) for RYAN stock has bolted higher this year, though it's still below the peak of the latest cup base. A rising RS line, the blue line in the chart shown, means that a stock is outperforming the S&P 500 index.  
RYAN 股票的[相对强弱线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-line-gives-crucial-clues-about-stocks-ready-to-make-big-gains/)今年已经走高，但仍低于最新杯底的峰值。上升的 RS 线，即图表中的蓝线，意味着一只股票的表现优于标准普尔 500 指数。


Among favorable technical signs, RYAN stock shows an [up/down volume ratio](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/top-stocks-under-accumulation-use-the-up-down-volume-ratio-to-find-the-best-prospects/) of 1.4. Ratings above 1 indicate upward price pressure on a stock.  
在有利的技术迹象中，RYAN 股票的[涨/跌成交量比率](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/top-stocks-under-accumulation-use-the-up-down-volume-ratio-to-find-the-best-prospects/)为 1.4。评级高于 1 表示股票的价格面临上行压力。


RYAN stock bears a superior Accumulation/Distribution Rating of A. The number of funds owning shares rose 14% in the latest June-ended quarter, a favorable sign, according to [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-ryan-specialty-holdings-ryan.aspx).  
RYAN 股票的累积/派发评级为 A。根据 [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-ryan-specialty-holdings-ryan.aspx) 的数据，在截至 6 月的最新季度中，持有股票的基金数量增长了 14%，这是一个有利的迹象。


This insurance play shows an 89 [Relative Strength Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/) out of a best-possible 99, up from 85 one week ago. The RS Rating means that Ryan Specialty has outperformed 89% of all stocks in IBD's database over the past year.  
这部保险游戏显示[相对强弱评级](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/)为 89 分（满分 99 分），高于一周前的 85 分。RS 评级意味着 Ryan Specialty 在过去一年中跑赢了 IBD 数据库中 89% 的股票。


Founded in 2010, the Chicago-based company went public in July 2021. Year to date, RYAN stock has surged nearly 59%. That includes a 7.5% earnings breakaway gap on Feb. 28 and a nearly 10% earnings surge to record highs on Aug. 2.  
这家总部位于芝加哥的公司成立于 2010 年，于 2021 年 7 月上市。年初至今，RYAN 的股票已飙升近 59%。这包括 2 月 28 日的 7.5% 的收益突破差距和 8 月 2 日近 10% 的收益飙升至历史新高。

The stock boasts a perfect [Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-research-growth-stocks/) of 99. The Composite Rating combines various technical and fundamental metrics into one easy-to-read score. The insurance company has delivered an unbroken string of quarterly earnings beats stretching back to the December 2022 period, according to FactSet. Earnings growth ranged from 28% to nearly 35% the last four quarters in a row. Ryan Specialty rang in sales growth of almost 19% to 23% over this same period. However, growth decelerated on both the top and bottom lines in the quarter ended June 30.  
根据 FactSet 的数据，自 2022 年 12 月以来，这家保险公司连续实现了一系列季度收益超预期。过去四个季度的盈利增长从 28% 到近 35% 不等。Ryan Specialty 在同一时期的销售额增长了近 19% 至 23%。然而，在截至 6 月 30 日的季度中，营收和利润的增长均有所放缓。


For the full year, analysts expect a 30.5% leap in earnings per share, slowing to a 22.4% gain in 2025.  
分析师预计全年每股收益将增长 30.5%，到 2025 年将放缓至 22.4%。


Amid a leadership reshuffle, Tim Turner took the helm as CEO on Tuesday. He succeeded founder Patrick Ryan, who has moved to an executive chairman role.  
在领导层改组中，蒂姆·特纳 （Tim Turner） 周二接任首席执行官一职。他接替了创始人 Patrick Ryan，后者已升任执行董事长一职。


Analysts on Wall Street remain on the sidelines regarding this specialty insurance play, FactSet shows. The consensus rating for RYAN stock is hold with a price target of 66.64, below where shares traded Tuesday.  
FactSet 显示，华尔街的分析师仍然对这种专业保险业务持观望态度。对 RYAN 股票的共识评级为持有，目标价为 66.64，低于周二的交易价格。
