**Howmet Aerospace** ([HWM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HWM)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) for Tuesday. The defense contractor has been one of the best performers in the S&P 500 this year amid its 87% rally to record highs. Now, Howmet Aerospace shares look actionable as they bounce off key moving averages. But investors should hold off for a couple of critical factors before jumping into the stock.  
**Howmet Aerospace** （[HWM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HWM)） 是周二[的每日 IBD 股票](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/)。这家国防承包商是今年标准普尔 500 指数中表现最好的公司之一，其 87% 的涨幅创下历史新高。现在，Howmet Aerospace 的股票看起来具有可操作性，因为它们从关键移动平均线反弹。但投资者在进入该股之前应该等待几个关键因素。




Pittsburgh-based Howmet Aerospace provides engine components and metal parts for the defense, aerospace and transportation industries. Among its key customers, Howmet supplies various bulkheads for **Lockheed Martin** ([LMT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=LMT)) F-35 Lightning II jets, as well as the blades for **RTX**'s ([RTX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RTX)) Pratt & Whitney PurePower Geared Turbofan Engine. The PurePower GTF engine is used by various commercial aircraft companies including the likes of **Airbus** ([EADSY](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=EADSY)) and **Boeing** ([BA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BA)).  
位于匹兹堡的Howmet Aerospace为国防、航空航天和运输行业提供发动机部件和金属部件。在其主要客户中，Howmet为**洛克希德·马丁**公司（[LMT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=LMT)）的F-35闪电II喷气机提供各种舱壁，以及**RTX**的（[RTX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RTX)）普惠PurePower齿轮涡轮风扇发动机的叶片。PurePower GTF发动机被各种商用飞机公司使用，包括**空客**（[EADSY](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=EADSY)）和**波音（**[BA）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BA)等公司。


The company averaged a 14% jump in sales over the first two quarters of the year, driven by growth in commercial aerospace revenue due to strong travel demand and an aging aircraft fleet. During Q2, CEO John Plant noted that the "extremely high" backlog at aircraft makers like Boeing has slowed the entire industry.  
该公司今年前两个季度的销售额平均增长了 14%，这得益于强劲的旅行需求和机队老化导致商业航空航天收入的增长。在第二季度，首席执行官约翰·普兰特 （John Plant） 指出，波音等飞机制造商的“极高”积压订单已经减缓了整个行业的发展。


Part of that backlog stems from Boeing's quality control issues and delivery delays, which have hamstrung the Dow Jones manufacturer throughout the year. Elsewhere, [Boeing and its machinist union on Monday](https://www.investors.com/news/boeing-strike-ends-union-worker-vote-ratify-deal/) agreed to a four-year contract and pay hike, which ends a nearly two-month strike.  
积压的部分原因在于波音的质量控制问题和交货延迟，这些问题全年都困扰着这家道琼斯制造商。在其他地方，[波音及其机械师工会周一](https://www.investors.com/news/boeing-strike-ends-union-worker-vote-ratify-deal/)同意签订一份为期四年的合同并加薪，结束了近两个月的罢工。

For the third quarter, FactSet expects Howmet Aerospace to report a 29% increase in earnings to 65 cents per share. Analysts see revenue rising about 12% to $1.85 billion.  
对于第三季度，FactSet 预计 Howmet Aerospace 的收益将增长 29%，至每股 65 美分。分析师预计收入将增长约 12%，达到 18.5 亿美元。


[Howmet, after its Q2 beat](https://www.investors.com/news/boeing-supplier-howmet-aerospace-stock-q2-earnings-guidance/), hiked its 2024 adjusted earnings per share guidance range to $2.53 to $2.57, up from $2.31 to $2.39. The company raised its 2024 revenue guidance to between $7.4 billion and $7.7 billion, compared to its prior outlook for $7.23 billion to $7.38 billion.  
[Howmet 在第二季度超出预期后](https://www.investors.com/news/boeing-supplier-howmet-aerospace-stock-q2-earnings-guidance/)，将其 2024 年调整后每股收益指导范围从 2.31 美元上调至 2.57 美元至 2.57 美元。该公司将 2024 年收入指引上调至 74 亿美元至 77 亿美元之间，而此前的预期为 72.3 亿美元至 73.8 亿美元。


FactSet analysts expect 2024 earnings to increase 40% to $2.59 per share on 12.4% revenue growth to $7.44 billion.  
FactSet 分析师预计 2024 年的收益将增长 40% 至每股 2.59 美元，收入增长 12.4% 至 74.4 亿美元。

HWM 股价今年上涨了近 87%，并在 10 月 21 日创下 106.93 的历史新高。


The advance has made it one of the best performers in the S&P 500, especially outside of artificial intelligence names. Including AI names, only **Vistra** ([VST](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=VST)), **Nvidia** ([NVDA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVDA)), **Palantir** ([PLTR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=PLTR)), **Constellation Energy** ([CEG](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CEG)), **Targa Resources** ([TRGP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TRGP)) and **United Airlines** ([UAL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UAL)) are outpacing Howmet.  
这一进步使其成为标准普尔 500 指数中表现最好的股票之一，尤其是在人工智能名称之外。包括 AI 名称在内，只有 **Vistra** （[VST](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=VST)）、**Nvidia** （[NVDA）、](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVDA)**Palantir** （[PLTR）、](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=PLTR)**Constellation Energy** （[CEG）、](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CEG)**Targa Resources** （[TRGP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TRGP)） 和**联合航空公司** （[UAL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UAL)） 的增长速度超过了 Howmet。


HWM stock climbed 1.4% on Tuesday. Shares have bounced off their [50-day moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-50-day-moving-average-when-to-buy-or-sell-growth-stocks/) and are trading just below their [21-day exponential line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/).  
HWM 股价周二上涨 1.4%。股价已从 [50 天移动平均线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-50-day-moving-average-when-to-buy-or-sell-growth-stocks/)反弹，交易价格略低于 [21 天指数线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/)。


A move above the 21-day line would make it actionable. But investors should likely wait until after earnings and the election are settled.  
突破 21 天线将使其具有可操作性。但投资者可能应该等到财报和选举尘埃落定之后。
