**Tempur Sealy** ([TPX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TPX)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/). Tempur Sealy stock moved toward a buy point after more than doubling in 2019. Analysts see Tempur Sealy earnings growth heating up in 2020 after a growth revival last year.




Rival mattress makers **Sleep Number** ([SNBR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=SNBR)) and **Purple Innovation** ([PRPL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=PRPL)) are working on bases. Upscale furniture retailer **RH** ([RH](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RH)) staged a bullish rebound Tuesday.


Both Tempur Sealy stock and RH stock are on [IBD Leaderboard](https://leaderboard.investors.com/#/leaders/leadersnearabuypoint).


Tempur Sealy Stock
------------------


Shares rose 0.5% to 87.65 in below-average volume on the [stock market today](http://investors.com/stock-market-today). Tempur Sealy stock has formed a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-patterns-flat-base-dull-trade-positive-action/) with a 92.33 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/). Aggressive investors might see an early entry at 89.86.


The flat base, going back to Nov. 1, is part of a [base-on-base formation](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/smart-chart-reading-use-the-base-on-base-pattern-as-springboard-for-big-profits/). The mattress retailer found support at a gently rising [50-day line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/when-to-sell-stocks-big-break-below-50-day-line-can-mark-end-of-a-huge-run/) in recent sessions, according to [MarketSmith analysis](https://www.investors.com/product/marketsmith/). Tempur Sealy has been riding an air cushion above the 200-day line for the past 12 months.


Tempur Sealy stock hit a record high of 92.33 on Nov. 1 following a strong third-quarter earnings beat.


However, the [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/a-stock-breakout-specialty-tool-the-relative-strength-line/) has been trending slightly lower in its flat base, as Tempur Sealy stock consolidated while the broad market ran higher. The RS line had a strong 2019 as Tempur Sealy stock surged 110%. The RS line, which tracks a stock's performance vs. the S&P 500, is the blue line in the chart provided.



Tempur Sealy Earnings
---------------------


Tempur Sealy earnings growth has reached double digits in the last three quarters. Sales growth has accelerated slowly for the past six quarters, going from -3% to to 13%.


The company reports Q4 results on Feb. 13. Analysts expect Tempur Sealy earnings per share to jump 31% to $1.18 as sales rise 14% to $771 million, according to Zacks Investment Research.


For 2020, Wall Street expects Tempur Sealy earnings per share to jump 46% to $5.55. Revenue is seen rising 16% to $3.49 billion.


That would compare with Tempur Sealy's three-year EPS growth rate of -10% and three-year sales growth rate of -4%. Adjusted annual earnings per share fell in 2017 and 2018.


The Kentucky-based company's new Breeze line of mattresses, which stay cooler, has seen strong demand. It also has seen "broad-based increases in demand above our expectations for both Tempur-Pedic and Sealy all around the world," management said on Oct. 31 of last year.


[Tempur Sealy also said](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2019/10/31/tempur-sealy-international-inc-tpx-q3-2019-earning.aspx) it benefited from new lines of distribution and "anti-dumping" duties against China matress rivals.


Tempur Sealy stock has a strong 90 [Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-research-growth-stocks/). Its [EPS Rating](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/find-evaluate-stocks/exclusive-ratings/), which measures a company's ability to accelerate profit, is 82.

Rival Mattress Retail Stocks
----------------------------


The maker of Tempur-Pedic and Sealy beds has found a winning formula in going high-tech, high-end and higher profit. [Tempur Sealy stock](https://www.investors.com/research/the-new-america/tempur-sealy-stock-wakes-deal-mattress-firm/) has followed.


A still-robust economy has also shored up the housing and home furnishings markets.


Tempur Sealy stock jumped 15% in June 2019 after ending a bitter, years-long dispute with customer Mattress Firm. It has also benefited from a new pact with **Big Lots** ([BIG](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BIG)).


But in the highly fragmented mattress business, Tempur Sealy confronts a number of upstarts, including Purple Innovation, which hawks more affordable "bed in a box" mattresses. Meanwhile, **Sleep Number**has attracted customers with [smart beds loaded with sleep-tracking technology](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/click/ces-2020-sleep-number-debuts-latest-smart-beds/). Casper Sleep recently filed for an IPO.


Tempur Sealy sells products in more than 100 countries. It derives roughly 29% of its net sales from outside the U.S.


Other Mattress, Furniture Stocks
--------------------------------


The Household/Office Furniture industry is ranked No. 76 out of 197 groups tracked by IBD. Tempur Sealy stock is rated No. 1 out of 17 stocks in that group. Purple Innovation stock is No. 3.


Purple Innovation stock closed flat at 10.70 after rising to 11.05 intraday, a 22-month high. Shares are just below an 11.04 buy point. But overall trading volume in Purple Innovation stock is rather thin.


The Retail-Home Furnishings industry group — which includes Sleep Number stock, RH stock and **Williams Sonoma** ([WSM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=WSM)) — currently holds the No. 16 spot among the 197 industry groups. RH stock is No. 1 in that group, Wiliams Sonoma stock is No. 2, and Sleep Number stock is No. 3, according to [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nyse-r-h-rh.aspx).


Sleep Number stock is basing around the 50-day line. Shares rose 0.45% to 47.39 on Tuesday. Sleep Number stock has a 90 Composite Rating and a 95 EPS Rating. But Sleep Number stock has had a number of failed breakouts over the past year.


RH stock jumped 3.6% to 223.36 Tuesday. Shares are rebounding from their 10-week moving average and breaking a short downtrend. RH stock has near-perfect Composite and EPS Ratings of 98.
