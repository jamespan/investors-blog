**NetEase** ([NTES](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NTES)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the Chinese video game publisher has seen its shares climb after an upbeat second-quarter earnings report. NTES stock is approaching a buy point.




On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/), NTES stock rose 1.6% to 492.89. Earlier in the session, it climbed as high as 498.82. It is nearing a proper [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) of 503.37 out of a third-stage [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-patterns-flat-base-dull-trade-positive-action/), according to [IBD MarketSmith charts](https://www.investors.com/product/marketsmith/?artProdLink=MarketSmith).


On Monday, NTES stock cleared an early-entry buy point of 487.21.


On Aug. 13, the Hangzhou, China-based company easily beat Wall Street's targets for the [June quarter](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/click/netease-earnings-q2-2020-ntes-stock/). It earned an adjusted $5.64 per American depositary share on sales of $2.57 billion in the June quarter. Analysts expected NetEase earnings of $4.58 per U.S. share on sales of $2.45 billion. On a year-over-year basis, NetEase earnings rose 28% while sales increased 22%, according to MarketSmith data.

Online Games, Music Streaming Drive Q2 Growth
---------------------------------------------


NetEase develops and operates popular PC and mobile games in China such as "Fantasy Westward Journey" and "Onmyoji." It also distributes games in China for **Activision Blizzard** ([ATVI](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ATVI)), **Microsoft**'s ([MSFT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MSFT))Â Mojang and others. In addition to games, NetEase offers online education, music streaming and other internet services.


Online game services and NetEase Cloud Music drove most of the company's growth in the second quarter.


NTES Stock Ranks No. 1 In Video Game Group
------------------------------------------


NTES stock ranks first out of 12 stocks in IBD's Computer Software-Gaming industry group, according to the [IBD Stock Checkup tool](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-netease-inc-adr-ntes.aspx). It has a best-possible [IBD Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-research-growth-stocks/) of 99. IBD's Composite Rating combines five separate proprietary ratings into one easy-to-use rating. The best growth stocks have a Composite Rating of 90 or better.


The video game software group ranks No. 18 out of 197 industry groups tracked by IBD. Choosing highly rated stocks from leading industry groups in a confirmed stock market uptrend generally increases your chances of making profits in growth stocks.


Video game companies have done well during the coronavirus pandemic as people have spent more time at home.


NTES stock has an [IBD Relative Strength Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/) of 92 out of 99. That means it has outperformed 92% of stocks over the past 12 months.


New Blockbuster Games Set For Release
-------------------------------------


"NetEase remains one of our preferred stocks among gaming companies, given the potential new blockbuster games in second-half 2020," UBS analyst Angela Xu said in an Aug. 14 report.


She added, "We are encouraged by NetEase's strong Q2 mobile games revenue, up 21% year over year and 5% quarter over quarter, even as gamers returned to work/school. We are positive on favorable gaming industry trend post quarantine, and more importantly, NetEase's continued initiatives in games diversification/globalization, which will further drive potential upsides in second-half 2020 and onward."


Xu reiterated her buy rating on NTES stock with a 12-month price target of 550.
