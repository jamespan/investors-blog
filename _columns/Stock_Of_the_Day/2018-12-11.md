**Ubiquiti Networks** ([UBNT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UBNT)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/), a telecommunications company which is showing healthy stock action in a tough market.







The company provides wireless data communication products for enterprise and wireless broadband providers. It also sells wireless networking gear to rural internet service providers.


Ubiquiti stock has been holding gains smartly after shares [gapped out of a base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/breakaway-gap-the-art-of-the-breakout/) Nov. 9 on strong earnings. The stock continues to show exceptional [relative strength](https://www.investors.com/research/best-stocks-rising-relative-strength/) in a weak market. It's trading tightly and holding near highs. But the stock is currently extended more that 5% above a [proper entry point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/). A stock within 5% of a buy point is said to be in its buy range, or chase zone. A stock that has moved [more than 5% above an entry point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/nvidia-buy-range/) is considered extended.


Ubiquiti stock broke out of a six-week [cup-without-handle base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/investing-202-why-some-great-cup-bases-dont-form-a-handle/) following its [quarterly earnings report](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/ubiquiti-stock-ubiquiti-earnings/) on Nov. 9. Revenue rose 15% from the year-ago period to $283 million. That smashed analyst estimates for $270.6 million. Adjusted earnings jumped 27% to $1.17 a share, topping estimates for $1.05.

Ubiquiti Stock Soars
--------------------


Following the earnings report, Ubiquiti stock soared 17.5% and blew past a buy point of 101.43. Ubiquiti stock closed at 108.52, down 0.4% on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/). The stock is up about 52% this year.


The company held its initial public offering in 2011, pricing shares at 15.


Following the quarterly earnings report, BMO Capital Markets analyst Tim Long raised his price target on Ubiquiti stock to 99 from 80.


"Ubiquiti employs a disruptive pricing model and has built a loyal and engaged user community that has helped drive adoption of its platform even without a conventional sales force," Long wrote in a note to clients.


Ubiquiti is a unique player in the wireless connectivity market, taking share historically from the traditional vendors through its low-cost value proposition.


Strong Relative Strength
------------------------


Ubiquiti stock currently holds a strong [Relative Strength rating](https://www.investors.com/research/best-stocks-rising-relative-strength/) of 98 out of a possible 99. The rating measures a stock's price performance over the last 12 months. The best stocks tend to have an 80 or better RS rating as they launch a new climb.


Ubiquiti also has an IBD Composite Rating of 99, the highest possible. It's currently rated No. 17 on the [IBD 50 list](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/ibd-50/) of leading growth stocks showing strong relative price strength and top-notch fundamentals base.


A report from [Zacks Investment Research](https://www.zacks.com/stock/news/342319/is-ubiquiti-networks-ubnt-outperforming-other-computer-and-technology-stocks-this-year) says it expects Ubiquiti to continue its solid performance.
