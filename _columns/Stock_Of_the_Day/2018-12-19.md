**Mellanox Technologies** ([MLNX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MLNX)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/), as the fabless chipmaker has risen on strong fundamentals and takeover talk. Mellanox stock avoided the recent tech sell-off.




On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/), Mellanox stock rose 2.4% to 91.04. It has formed a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/common-patterns-3/) with a [buy point](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/when-to-buy/) of 99.24 over the past six weeks.


The stock's [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/research/best-stocks-rising-relative-strength/) has climbed to new highs amid the market correction. Mellanox currently has an [IBD Relative Strength Rating](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/find-evaluate-stocks/exclusive-ratings/) of 95, meaning it has outperformed 95% of stocks in key metrics over the past 12 months.


Mellanox Technologies is currently the [No. 3 stock](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-mellanox-technologies-mlnx.aspx) in IBD's Electronics-Semiconductor Fabless industry group, which includes 33 companies. The No. 1 stock, **Integrated Device Technology** ([IDTI](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=IDTI)), is being acquired by Renesas Electronics. **Xilinx** ([XLNX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=XLNX)), a key player in data centers and 5G wireless base stations, is the No. 2 stock in the group.

Xilinx, Microsoft Reportedly Pursuing Mellanox
----------------------------------------------


CNBC reported in [late October](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/mellanox-technologies-takeover-talk/) that Mellanox hired a financial advisor to investigate a sale after receiving takeover interest from at least two companies. Xilinx is one of the suitors, [CNBC said](https://www.cnbc.com/2018/11/07/xilinx-working-with-barclays-to-buy-mellanox-possible-december-deal.html) last month.


On Wednesday, [news reports](https://www.timesofisrael.com/microsoft-mulls-acquisition-of-israeli-chipmaker-mellanox-report/) said **Microsoft** ([MSFT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MSFT)) is interested in acquiring Mellanox and has hired Goldman Sachs to negotiate a deal. Microsoft is a major buyer of Mellanox products.


Israel-based Mellanox makes high-performance, end-to-end interconnect systems for data-center servers and storage computers. It supplies ethernet and InfiniBand systems to enterprise data centers, internet cloud computing, network security, telecom and financial services.


Mellanox Stock: Rapid Earnings Growth
-------------------------------------


On Oct. 24, Mellanox posted better-than-expected results for the third quarter and gave upbeat guidance. On a year-over-year basis, earnings per share rose 87% while sales climbed 24% in the September quarter. Its earnings growth in the first and second quarters was 238% and 184%, respectively.


For the current quarter, Mellanox guided to revenue of $285 million vs. Wall Street's target of $280.6 million.


On a [conference call with analysts](https://seekingalpha.com/article/4214114-mellanox-technologies-mlnx-q3-2018-results-earnings-call-transcript?part=single) to discuss third-quarter results, Chief Executive Eyal Waldman touted the company's leadership in adapters running at 25 gigabits per second and above.


"We remain confident that we are in the early stages of the transition to 25-gigabit-per-second and above speeds, which translates to a multiyear runway of growth," he said. "We expect the majority of that transition to occur over the next several years with analysts forecasting high-speed 25 gigabit and above adapters will represent more than 50% of the market by 2021."
