**Fifth Third Bancorp** ([FITB](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FITB)) is Tuesday's [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/), as the banking stock broke out above a buy point amid the recent sector rally. FITB shares angled higher Tuesday.




The Cincinnati, Ohio-based bank climbed as segments of the banking sector rallied against a sluggish market. Superregional banks and West/Southwest regional banks were two of IBD's five top-performing industries on Tuesday. **PNC Financial** ([PNC](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=PNC)) rallied more than 5% for the week on Tuesday.


**Huntington Bancshares** ([HBAN](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HBAN)) broke out with a 4% gain. **Morgan Stanley** ([MS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MS)) popped nearly 5% Monday and Tuesday, retaking its 50-day moving average for the first time since January.


**KeyCorp** ([KEY](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=KEY)) rallied 5.2% Monday and Tuesday, also edging near a buy point.


Meanwhile, **BlackRock** ([BLK](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BLK)) was the [IBD Stock Of The Day on Monday](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/blackrock-stock-bitcoin-prices-near-all-time-high-bitcoin-etfs/).


IBD added Fifth Third to its [IBD Leaderboard](https://leaderboard.investors.com/) portfolio on Tuesday.


Fifth Third Financials
----------------------


Fifth Third operates in three segments: commercial banking, consumer and small business banking as well as wealth and asset management. The company reported mixed fourth-quarter earnings on Jan. 19, beating revenue expectations but missing on earnings, as EPS tumbled 29%. Wall Street predicts earnings of $3.19 per share in 2024, down 1% vs. 2023, with sales hitting $8.64 billion, also down 1% compared to last year.


Looking further out, analysts forecast around 10% earnings growth in 2025 with revenue hitting $9 billion.


FITB shares closed out 2023 by gaining more than 40% in the final two months of the year. The stock dipped less than 1% in January before booking another monthly advance in February.


Fifth Third Bancorp Stock Performance
-------------------------------------


FITB shares jumped 2.5% to 36.35 in below average volume during Tuesday's [stock market action](https://www.investors.com/news/stock-market-today-stock-market-news/?). So far in February, Fifth Third Bancorp has advanced around 5.8%, on pace for a second consecutive monthly gain.



FITB is breaking out above an official 35.73 [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/apple-stock-set-up-proper-buy-point-before-big-rally/) from a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-a-flat-base-skechers-stock-skx/) on a weekly chart, according to [MarketSurge](https://marketsurge.investors.com/). The stock had been attempting to top resistance just above 35 since late December.


Fifth Third soared out of a [double-bottom base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-charts-difference-between-double-bottom-flat-base/) in late November 2023, rising 25% before leveling off.


Fifth Third Bancorp has an 82 [Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stocks-to-buy-and-watch-ibd-composite-rating-top-growth-stocks/) out of 99, an 86 [Relative Strength Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/) and a 67 [EPS Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/eps-rating-is-key-to-picking-great-stocks/).
