**Inphi** ([IPHI](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=IPHI)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the chipmaker soared to a record high on Tuesday, amid a good day for chip stocks overall.




Inphi stock jumped 4.7% to close at 78.98 on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/). Shares broke out of a [cup-with-handle](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/how-to-buy/common-patterns-1/) base with a buy point of 76.95. The buy range extends to 80.80.


While the stock market mostly remained mixed Tuesday, following renewed threats of retaliation from Iran, [the Nasdaq found support](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-edges-down-iran-threat-chipmakers-gains/) from the chip sector. Top gainers among the Nasdaq 100 included **Micron Technology** ([MU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MU)) and **Microchip Technology** ([MCHP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MCHP)), both up more than 5%.


Inphi reported [third-quarter earnings](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/inphi-earnings-q3-2019-inphi-stock/) in late October that beat estimates. Revenue climbed 21% to $94 million, vs. estimates of $89 million. Adjusted earnings jumped 50% to 45 cents a share, beating view of 37 cents. Inphi stock surged 8.5% in reaction to the earnings report.

For the current quarter, Inphi expects revenue to jump about 154% from the year-ago period to $99.8 million. That's based on the midpoint of its guidance. It expects earnings of 45 cents per share, the same as last year.


Inphi Stock: Founded 19 Years Ago
---------------------------------


Based in Santa Clara, Calif., Inphi was founded 19 years ago.


The company makes chip components and optical subsystems it calls "data movement interconnects." Customers for its products include cloud computing providers, telecom firms and data center operators. Inphi also serves developers of optical modules for high-speed communications.


A number of new applications are gathering momentum such as cloud services, big data and the internet of things. This also includes artificial intelligence, autonomous vehicles, augmented reality and new media. These applications are driving an unprecedented explosion in data and traffic, fueling demand for Inphi solutions.


In its promos, the company claims, "We move big data fast, around the globe, with high quality and reliability."


Inphi Checkup Results
---------------------


The IBD [Stock Checkup Tool](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-tesla-inc-tsla.aspx) shows that Inphi has an [IBD Composite Rating](https://education.investors.com/financial-dictionary/general/composite-rating-cma--smartselect-rgr-) of 98 out of a best-possible 99. The rating means Inphi stock currently outperforms 98% of all stocks in terms of the most important fundamental and technical stock-picking criteria


It has a [Relative Strength Rating](https://education.investors.com/financial-dictionary/general/relative-price-strength--op-rs-cp--rating-or-relative-strength) of 97 out of 99. The rating tracks market leadership by showing how a stock's price movement over the past 52 weeks measures up against that of the other stocks.


Inphi has a strong [EPS Rating](https://education.investors.com/financialdictionary.aspx?mode=1&term=EPS%2fRating&termid=1) of 94, which compares quarterly and annual earnings-per-share growth with all other stocks. The stock trades above its [50-day moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/when-to-sell-stocks-big-break-below-50-day-line-can-mark-end-of-a-huge-run/) while its [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/a-stock-breakout-specialty-tool-the-relative-strength-line/) is at a new high.


Inphi Stock: High Group Ranking
-------------------------------


Inphi stock also ranks No. 2 in IBD's Electronics-Semiconductor Fabless industry group. The top stock in the group is **Mellanox Technologies** ([MLNX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MLNX)), which is being acquired by **Nvidia** ([NVDA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVDA)). **Monolithic Power Systems** ([MPWR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MPWR)) is No. 3.


The fabless chip group ranks No. 7 out of 197 industry groups.
