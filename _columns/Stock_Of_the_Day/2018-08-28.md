**ASGN Incorporated** ([ASGN](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ASGN)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/). After breaking out in late July on double-digit quarterly growth following a four-month flat base, ASGN stock has remained in a tight trading range, a bullish sign.




On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/), ASGN stock dipped 0.3%, to 91.68. At the same time, the broader communication services and staffing industry group lifted a fraction. The 23-company group is ranked No. 52 out of 197 groups tracked by Investor's Business Daily.


Staffing And IT Services
------------------------


[ASGN provides staffing](https://www.asgn.com/about-us) and IT services for several industries, including information technology, engineering, health information, telecommunications and other technical disciplines.

The stock has an [IBD Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/ibd-data-stories/companies-now-outperforming-95-of-all-stocks/) of 97 out of a best-possible 99, meaning it performs in the top 2% of all stocks in terms of key growth metrics. In its group, only executive search firm **Heidrick and Struggles International** ([HSII](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HSII)) has a higher CR at 98.


Shares broke out of a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/when-to-buy-the-basics-of-a-flat-base-a-super-growth-stock-pattern/) at 86.23 on July 25, after ASGN issued its [second-quarter report](https://investors.asgn.com/press-release/asgn-incorporated-reports-second-quarter-2018-results). ASGN stock is now 6.7% extended from that [buy point](https://www.investors.com/category/stock-lists/stocks-near-a-buy-zone/). Its new buy point is now 94.35. The broader group of communication and staffing stocks has stayed flat over that time period.


ASGN Stock And Growth
---------------------


ASGN's adjusted earnings have climbed by at least double digits for the past 12 straight quarters. In the second quarter ended in June, ASGN generated adjusted profit of $1.11 per share, rising 42% year over year.


The firm also posted its best quarterly sales growth in two years. ASGN reported $878.5 million in sales, growing 34%. That rebounded from six quarters of single-digit growth.


ASGN's largest segment, Apex, accounted for 64.6% of total revenue and grew 12.9% year over year. Its Oxford and ECS units each accounted for 17.7% chunks of total sales in the quarter. Oxford sales rose 3.3% while ESC sales grew 7.1% on a pro forma basis.


Strong U.S. Economy
-------------------


Chief Executive Peter Dameris credited strong growth in the U.S. economy for the company's strong second-quarter performance. Commercial and government organizations are investing in their businesses and services, he said in a prepared statement.


"Against this positive economic backdrop, ASGN executed well and we were able to grow substantially faster than the published industry growth rates for the 18th consecutive quarter," he said. "We expect our size and service offerings will continue to position ASGN to generate solid-above market growth in the quarters ahead.


After its quarterly report in July, Robert W. Baird analysts kept a neutral rating on ASGN stock. Credit Suisse analysts initiated coverage with a neutral rating and 92 price target.
