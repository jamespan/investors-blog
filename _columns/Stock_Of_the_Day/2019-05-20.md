**Ulta Beauty** ([ULTA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ULTA)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as it flashes a bullish indicator while seeking support close to record highs amid a stock market correction. Ulta Beauty is close to forming a new base, while it has been trading tightly for the past few weeks.




The specialty beauty products retailer and salon has outperformed the retail sector strong for years, with solid same-store sales growth, solid store expansion and robust e-commerce growth. Ulta Beauty offers both prestige and mass products under one roof. It also offers salon services. The company operates 1,174 stores and has increasing its total square footage by 9.2% year over year.

Ulta Beauty Stock Technical Analysis
------------------------------------


Shares rose 1.6% to 347.89 on [the stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/). Ulta Beauty stock found support at its [50-day line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/50-day-moving-average/), a key technical benchmark. It has been struggling to hold firm above this level for the past week or so. A strong rebound off the 50-day line would offer a buying opportunity for existing holders or aggressive investors taking a new position.


Ulta Beauty stock also is working on aÂ [new flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-patterns-flat-base-dull-trade-positive-action/), with a buy point of 359.79.


Ulta Beauty's [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/a-stock-breakout-specialty-tool-the-relative-strength-line/) has been on an upward trend since February 2018, and it has been progressing well so far this year. The RS line has been edging higher since May 1, near record levels, as the S&P 500 and overall stock market have declined. This is a bullish sign.



Fundamentals Solid For Ulta Beauty Stock
----------------------------------------


Ulta Beauty stock has an [IBD Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/the-ibd-composite-rating/) of 93, just two points shy of the 95 benchmark for [CAN SLIM stocks](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/can-slim/). The [Stock Checkup Tool](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-ulta-beauty-inc-ulta.aspx) shows earnings are strong, with EPS growing by an average of 31% over the past three quarters. Ulta Beauty earnings has also swelled by 29% over the past three years.


One concern is that overall sales growth has trended down over the past year, to 10% in the latest quarter.


CAN SLIM connoisseurs are also always on the lookout for pace-setting stocks, with the L in the acronym standing for Leaders. Here again Ulta Beauty stock shines, as it holds first place in the [Retail-Specialty Industry Group](https://www.investors.com/data-tables/industry-group-rankings-may-17-2019/).


Analyst Rates Ulta Beauty Stock
-------------------------------


Jefferies analyst Stephanie Wissink was rating Ulta Beauty stock as a buy with a 357 target after it defied flattish industry trends in Q4. She sees scope for the firm to gain continue to grow and snatch share.


"Credit card sign-ups were higher and the services platform looks ripe for greater utilization, backed by tech and execution enhancements," she said in a research note. "The pipeline for continued distinctiveness is in place and we have a higher degree of confidence in ongoing (and potentially accelerating) share capture post-Q4."
