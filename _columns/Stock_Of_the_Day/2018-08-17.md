**Vocera Communications** ([VCRA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=VCRA)) is the [IBD Stock of the Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as shares flirt with a second breakout in two months following massive earnings growth in the second quarter.




On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/), Vocera stock advanced 0.3%, to 31.99. Shares began forming a flat base in July with a buy point at 33.27.


This could be Vocera's second breakout in two months. Shares began a lengthy consolidation in October 2017 and broke out in early July after topping a [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) at 32.33. After that, the stock almost immediately began forming its current flat base.

Vocera makes a platform for [health care clinical communication](https://www.vocera.com/about-us) and workflow. This helps to reduce "alarm fatigue, speed up staff response times and improve patient care, safety and experience," the firm says. It also has a foothold in luxury homes, nuclear facilities, libraries and retail.


It trades in a strong industry group of communication services for health care. Shares of group, which includes a total of 13 stocks, are up a collective 13% year to date. The group is ranked No. 18 out of the 197 groups Investor's Business Daily tracks, making a bullish climb from No. 173 six months ago.


Vocera stock also has an [IBD Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/find-evaluate-stocks/exclusive-ratings/) of 93 out of a best-possible 99, meaning it trades in the top 7% of all stocks in terms of key growth metrics. Other top performers include **HMS Holdings** ([HMSY](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HMSY)), **Cotiviti Holdings** ([COTV](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=COTV)) and **National Research** ([NRC](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NRC)), with Composite Ratings of 98, 97 and 94, respectively.


Strong Earnings Growth
----------------------


Shares of Vocera have climbed amid major earnings growth. Adjusted earnings per share have topped estimates for the past 16 quarters. In the [recent second quarter](https://www.vocera.com/press-release/vocera-announces-second-quarter-revenue-427-million), Vocera's adjusted profit grew 350% to 9 cents per share.


Vocera also has managed to grow its sales each quarter for the past 13 periods. In the quarter ended in June, Vocera's sales grew 8% year over year to $42.7 million.


In the [current third quarter](https://www.vocera.com/press-release/vocera-announces-third-quarter-revenue-423-million), analysts polled by Zacks Investment Research call for Vocera to report adjusted income of 11 cents per share. That would be flat on a year-over-year basis. Revenue of $46 million would increase 8.7% on a year-over-year basis.
