**Arista Networks** ([ANET](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ANET)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the computer networking firm trades just above its 50-day line and has entered a new buy area. ANET stock has climbed 53% in 2023 and got a big boost after Arista's second-quarter earnings report in late July.




Arista Networks is on the [IBD Leaderboard.](https://leaderboard.investors.com/#/leaders/leadersnearabuypoint) Further, the firm has shown strong earnings and revenue growth in recent quarters. The Santa Clara, Calif.-based company provides cloud networking software and hardware in the data center market.


On the stock market today, ANET added 0.6% to close at 186.03. The stock's rebound above the 50-day line places ANET in a new buy area. But a market outlook still in correction indicates a need for caution.


ANET Stock: AI Outlook
----------------------


Some analysts view Arista Networks as a longer-term [artificial intelligence](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/artificial-intelligence-stocks/) play.


Arista sells computer network switches that speed up communications between computer servers in "hyperscale" data centers that support companies and organizations with huge processing demands.


As AI adoption grows, so will workloads at internet data centers. That could require more network bandwidth to process AI workloads.


Arista's two biggest customers are major players in AI: Facebook-parent **Meta Platforms** ([META](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=META)) and **Microsoft** ([MSFT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MSFT))


The company highlighted the role of AI in its strategy on its second-quarter earnings call on July 31.


"The AI opportunity is exciting," said Chief Executive Jayshree Ullal. "As our largest cloud customers review their classic cloud and AI networking plans, Arista is adapting to these changes, thereby doubling down on our investments in AI."


Second-Quarter Earnings Beat
----------------------------


Meanwhile, Arista's second-quarter earnings climbed 46% to $1.58 per share, which [beat analyst estimates](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/anet-stock-arista-earnings-q22023/) of $1.44 per share. Revenue jumped 39% to $1.46 billion, also ahead of estimates of $1.38 billion.


ANET stock jumped just under 20% on Aug. 1, the first trading day after its earnings report.


For the September-ending quarter, Arista has guided for revenue in a range of $1.45 billion to $1.5 billion, representing about 25% year-over-year growth at the middle of the range.


Arista will report third-quarter earnings on Oct. 30. Analysts expect the company to post earnings of $1.58 per share, representing 26.4% growth, according to FactSet.


Analysts are largely bullish on the stock heading into the report. Arista holds a buy or overweight ratings from 16 analysts, compared to nine hold ratings and one sell or underweight view, according to FactSet.


ANET Stock Technical Ratings
----------------------------


Notably, Arista Networks is the top-rated stock in IBD's Computer-Networking industry group, according to [IBD Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/). However, that group has underperformed overall, and is ranked 108 out of the 197 industry groups tracked by IBD.


Further, ANET stock boasts a near-perfect IBD Composite Rating of 98 out of 99, according to IBD Stock Checkup. The score combines five separate proprietary ratings into one. The best growth stocks have a Composite Rating of 90 or better.


ANET's Relative Strength Rating is 95 out of a best-possible 99. The rating measures how a stock's price movement over the last 52 weeks compares with that of other stocks in IBD's database.


ANET stock has an Accumulation/Distribution Rating of B-. The rating analyzes price and volume changes in a stock over the past 13 weeks of trading. Its current rating indicates more funds are buying than selling.
