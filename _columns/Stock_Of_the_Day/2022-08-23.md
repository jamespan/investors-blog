Earnings Report A Catalyst For Stock
------------------------------------


Monolithic Power Systems got a big boost from its [beat-and-raise earnings report](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/mpwr-stock-monolithic-power-systems-trounces-q2-targets/) late on Aug. 1. MPWR stock rocketed 9.4% the day after the report.


The Kirkland, Wash.-based company's adjusted earnings surged 80% to $3.25 a share in the second quarter. Its sales increased 57% to $461 million. Robust sales of power-management chips for enterprise data, storage and computing applications drove the company's second-quarter beat.


For the current quarter, Monolithic forecast revenue of $480 million to $500 million. The midpoint of $490 million was well above Wall Street's target of $458 million. Its sales in the year-earlier period were $323.5 million.


Monolithic Power Systems makes semiconductor-based electronics power control systems.


In the second quarter, the enterprise data segment was its fastest growing business, up 118% year over year to $65.2 million. That segment represented 14.2% of the company's revenue vs. 10.2% in the year-earlier period.
