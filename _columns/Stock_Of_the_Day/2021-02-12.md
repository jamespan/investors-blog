**Marvell Technology Group** ([MRVL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MRVL)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the chipmaker shows strength ahead of its fiscal fourth-quarter earnings report. MRVL stock flashed a buy signal on Friday.




In afternoon trading on the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/), MRVL stock rose 3.3% to close at 53.82. On Thursday, MRVL stock climbed above its [21-day exponential moving average](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/what-is-the-21-day-exponential-moving-average/), creating a buying opportunity, based on [IBD trading guidelines](https://www.investors.com/ibd-university/). On Friday, it cleared a short downtrend in another bullish move.


MRVL stock found support above its [10-week moving average line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-buy-stocks-why-the-10-week-moving-average-offers-new-entry-points/) after a two-week pullback starting in late January, according to [IBD MarketSmith](https://www.investors.com/product/marketsmith/?artProdLink=MarketSmith) charts. The buy range from the current action extends to 54.13, or 10% above its 10-week moving average.

Marvell Earnings Report Due March 3
-----------------------------------


The Santa Clara, Calif.-based company plans to release its fiscal fourth-quarter results on March 3. Analysts expect Marvell earnings of 29 cents a share, up 71% year over year, on sales of $794.7 million, up 11%. If it hits those numbers, it would mark its fourth straight quarter of accelerating earnings growth. The company is benefiting from a cyclical upturn in semiconductor sales.


Marvell makes data infrastructure chips used in cloud computing, automotive, communications and other applications.


MRVL stock jumped 3.2% on Dec. 14 on news that it would be [added to the Nasdaq 100 index](https://www.marvell.com/company/newsroom/marvell-added-to-nasdaq-100-index.html) starting Dec. 21.


On Dec. 15, Marvell announced that its planned acquisition of **Inphi** ([IPHI](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=IPHI)) had cleared a [U.S. regulatory milestone](https://www.marvell.com/company/newsroom/marvell-announces-expiration-of-hart-scott-rodino-waiting-period.html). Marvell expects the [$10 billion deal](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/semiconductor-stocks-marvell-technology-inphi-merge/) to close by the second half of 2021.
