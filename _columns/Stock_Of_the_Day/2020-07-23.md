**Qorvo** ([QRVO](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=QRVO)) is the [IBD Stock Of The Day](https://www.investors.com/category/research/ibd-stock-of-the-day/) as the wireless-chip maker is seen profiting from the 5G cellular transition. Qorvo stock broke out on Thursday.




On the [stock market today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-market-trends-best-stocks-buy-watch/), Qorvo surged past a [buy point](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-reading-basics-how-a-buy-point-marks-a-time-of-opportunity/) of 119.04 out of a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/chart-patterns-flat-base-dull-trade-positive-action/), according to [IBD MarketSmith charts](https://www.investors.com/product/marketsmith/?artProdLink=MarketSmith). But the stock retreated, and ended trading below that point, ultimately down 0.2% to 118.15.


Thursday's action follows the stock's June 2 breakout from a [cup-with-handle base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/the-basics-how-to-analyze-a-stocks-cup-with-handle/) at a buy point of 108.88.


Qorvo stock hit a record high of 122.37 on Jan. 30 after the company reported [better-than-expected December-quarter earnings](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/semiconductor-stocks-cirrus-logic-surges/).

Volatile Around Quarterly Earnings Reports
------------------------------------------


Qorvo stock has been [volatile around earnings reports](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/should-you-buy-stock-ahead-of-earnings/). It also spiked May 8 after posting [March-quarter results](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/inphi-stock-jumps-beat-raise-quarterly-report/). So investors should be cautious ahead of the company's June-quarter earnings, due out Wednesday.


In a bullish sign, the [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/a-stock-breakout-specialty-tool-the-relative-strength-line/) for Qorvo stock is carving fresh highs as it outperforms the broader market. Qorvo has an [IBD Relative Strength Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-rating-stock-chart-analysis-helps-pick-outstanding-growth-stocks/) of 87. That means it has outperformed 87% of stocks over the past 12 months.



Qorvo stock has an [IBD Composite Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-research-growth-stocks/) of 93 out of 99, according to the [IBD Stock Checkup tool](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/nasdaq-qorvo-inc-qrvo.aspx). The Composite Rating scores a stock's key growth metrics against all other stocks regardless of industry group.


Greensboro, N.C.-based Qorvo makes radio-frequency chips for wireless communications.


Qorvo isn't the only semiconductor company benefiting from the 5G wireless rollout. Others include **Broadcom** ([AVGO](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AVGO)), **Qualcomm** ([QCOM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=QCOM)) and **Skyworks Solutions** ([SWKS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=SWKS)).


Qorvo Stock Gets Price-Target Hike
----------------------------------


On Tuesday, Needham analyst Rajvindra Gill reiterated his buy rating on Qorvo stock and raised his price target to 160 from 150. He sees Qorvo gaining market share in cellular base stations with its gallium nitride semiconductors, particularly in China. Plus, Qorvo is benefiting from the transition to Wi-Fi 6 products, Gill said.


Qorvo also should get a boost from 5G smartphone sales, Piper Sandler analyst Harsh Kumar said in a report Friday.


"We see Qorvo as a strong beneficiary from the 5G handset ramp beginning in the second half of 2020 and continuing for several more years," Kumar said in a note to clients. "The company has one of the most complete RF (radio-frequency) product sets."


Kumar rates Qorvo stock as overweight with a price target of 130.
