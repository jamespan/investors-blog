Fortress Transportation Resets Base Count, Nears All-Time High
--------------------------------------------------------------


Through the purchase and leasing of transportation equipment, Fortress Transportation looks to generate stable cash flow and earnings growth. Over the last two quarters, the company has generated EPS growth of 700% and 275%. For full year 2019, analysts expect earnings to rise 458%, followed by a 49% increase in 2020.


Since 2002, Fortress Transportation has acquired over $17 billion in transportation assets. As of September 2019, the company had total consolidated assets of $3.1 billion.


Fortress Transportation invests in several transportation industries, including aviation, energy and rail.


The company pays a strong dividend, currently yielding 6.6% on an annualized basis.


Fortress Transportation stock [reset its base count](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/counting-bases-is-a-crucial-skill-in-playing-long-winning-stock-rallys/) and is now building a first-stage pattern. The new consolidation is a [flat base](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/chart-reading-for-beginners-chart-patterns-cup-with-handle-double-bottom-flat-base/) showing a 20.33 entry.


On Thursday, the stock jumped 1.6% in volume more than 200% above average. It temporarily cleared the its buy point, but but edged lower to close below it, still ending the session in the upper half of the day's trading range. Fortress Transportation ended Friday's session with a 2.5% to close above its buy point. Preliminarly volume came in 348% above average.
