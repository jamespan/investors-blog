5G Stocks To Watch: Qorvo Mobilizes On Accelerating Growth
----------------------------------------------------------


Qorvo connects people, places and things through its expertise in RF technology, 5G and Wi-Fi networks. Key applications for the semiconductor industry leader include [5G cellular networks](https://www.investors.com/tag/telecom-stocks-to-watch-and-industry-news/), the [automotive industry](https://www.investors.com/news/automotive-industry-news-self-driving-cars-and-stocks-to-watch/), data storage, and [defense and aerospace](https://www.investors.com/news/defense-and-aerospace-stocks-to-watch-and-industry-news/).


In its latest-reported quarter, for fiscal 2021 Q2, Qorvo flashed acceleration in both sales and earnings. Revenue growth jumped from 2% to 31% in the latest quarter, while EPS growth showed a quantum leap from 10% to 60%.


For the current quarter, analysts expect a 43% rise in earnings. For the full fiscal year, consensus analyst estimates call for a 36% EPS increase.


Like Taiwan Semiconductor and Macom, Qorvo earns an A in [Stock Checkup](https://research.investors.com/stock-checkup/) for its [SMR Rating](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-find-top-stocks-2/), which tracks sales growth, profit margins and return on equity as a whole.


Qorvo sports a 27.3% annual pretax profit margin and 17.4% ROE. TSM stock comes in with a 36.4% profit margin and 20.9% ROE.


QRVO Stock Rides 5G To Top Of Buy Zone
--------------------------------------


After clearing its 171 buy point on Wednesday, Qorvo is adding to those gains in rising weekly volume. QRVO stock has now reached the top of its new buy zone.


Indicating stock market leadership, Qorvo's [relative strength line](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/relative-strength-line-gives-crucial-clues-about-stocks-ready-to-make-big-gains/) has hit a new 52-week high. Already trading well above its 50-day moving average, QRVO stock showed continued support as it jumped off its 21-day line on Jan. 5 on its way to the latest breakout.
