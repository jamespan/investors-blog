  Stocks Bounce As This Index Trounces Others, And A Bullish Reply To A Bearish Sign	股市反弹，因为该指数击败了其他指数，以及对看跌信号的看涨回应

Small caps shined Wednesday as the major indexes battled back from the prior session's stumble and showed that the stock market can brush aside an occasional day of heavy selling.  
周三，随着主要股指从前一交易日的跌跌撞撞中反击，小盘股大放异彩，并表明股市可以忽略偶尔的大量抛售。


The S&P 500 bounced 0.5% as financials and utilities performed best. The Nasdaq composite, which shed 1% Tuesday, regained a more moderate 0.3%. The PHLX Semiconductor Index made a mild gain, while software and other technology groups made up 11 of the 22 worst-performing industry groups Wednesday.  
标准普尔 500 指数反弹 0.5%，金融和公用事业表现最佳。纳斯达克综合指数周二下跌 1%，收复了较为温和的 0.3%。PHLX 半导体指数小幅上涨，而软件和其他科技股占周三表现最差的 22 个行业组中的 11 个。




The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 0.8% to a record closing high. Rebounds by **UnitedHealth Group** ([UNH](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UNH)) and **Visa** ([V](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=V)) contributed nearly 150 points to the Dow's 337-point gain.  
道琼斯工业平均指数上涨 0.8%，创下收盘新高。**UnitedHealth Group** （[UNH](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UNH)） 和 **Visa** （[V](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=V)） 的反弹为道琼斯指数的 337 点上涨贡献了近 150 点。


Volume fell on the Nasdaq and New York Stock Exchange, although breadth was positive. Keep in mind that Tuesday's volume was the highest so far this month.  
纳斯达克和纽约证券交易所的成交量下降，尽管广度为正。请记住，周二的交易量是本月迄今为止的最高水平。


Advancers topped decliners by about 3-to-1 on the Nasdaq and by about 13-to-4 on the NYSE, according to FactSet. The ratio of [52-week highs](https://www.investors.com/data-tables/new-high-list-oct-15-2024/) to new lows continues to favor bulls.  
根据 FactSet 的数据，纳斯达克的上涨股以约 3 比 1 的比分领先于下跌的股票，在纽约证券交易所以约 13 比 4 的比分领先于下跌的股票。[52 周高点](https://www.investors.com/data-tables/new-high-list-oct-15-2024/)与新低的比率继续有利于多头。


The IBD 50 index, a gauge of growth leaders, rose 0.5%. It extended a 2 1/2-month uptrend.  
衡量增长领导者的 IBD 50 指数上涨 0.5%。它延续了 2 1/2 个月的上升趋势。


Stock Market Responds To Distribution Day股市对分配日的反应
--------------------------------------------------


One day after the Nasdaq and S&P 500 logged a [distribution day](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-timing-the-subtle-sign-of-market-tops/), buyers quickly stepped in. The S&P 500 and Nasdaq didn't quite make up Tuesday's losses, but the Dow surpassed Monday's close of 43,065.  
在纳斯达克和标准普尔 500 指数记录[分销日](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-timing-the-subtle-sign-of-market-tops/)的一天后，买家迅速介入。标准普尔 500 指数和纳斯达克指数未能完全弥补周二的损失，但道琼斯指数超过了周一收盘价 43,065 点。


In a sign of stock market resilience, indexes have tended to recover soon from bouts of institutional-size selling i.e., distribution days.  
作为股市弹性的一个迹象，指数往往很快就会从一轮又一轮的机构规模抛售（即分销日）中恢复过来。


![](https://www.investors.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/MP101624.jpg)The Nasdaq responded to its distribution days on Sept. 20, Oct. 1, and Oct. 7 with rebounds within a few days, if not the very next session. The same can be said of the S&P 500's Sept. 17 stalling day and its distribution days on Sept. 18 and Oct. 7.  
纳斯达克指数在 9 月 20 日、10 月 1 日和 10 月 7 日的发行日做出了回应，几天内（如果不是下一个交易日的话）出现了反弹。标准普尔 500 指数 9 月 17 日的停滞日以及 9 月 18 日和 10 月 7 日的发行日也是如此。


That's not to downplay the importance of distribution days. They are especially helpful in spotting stealth selling even while the stock market is rising.  
这并不是要低估分发日的重要性。即使在股市上涨时，它们也特别有助于发现隐形抛售。


But the way the major indexes are behaving, there's reason for optimism. The current tally of four distribution days each on the Nasdaq and S&P is tolerable.  
但从主要指数的表现来看，我们有理由感到乐观。目前纳斯达克和标准普尔各有四个分发日的记录是可以接受的。


Other signs support the case for 80%-100% exposure, as IBD currently recommends. One of those is the re-emergence of small caps.  
其他迹象支持 IBD 目前建议的 80%-100% 暴露的情况。其中之一是小盘股的重新出现。


Small Caps Boost Stock Market小盘股提振股市
------------------------------------


The Russell 2000 made a 52-week closing high after the index climbed 1.6% Wednesday. It was the highest close since November 2o21, and the fourth consecutive gain.  
罗素 2000 指数周三上涨 1.6% 后创下 52 周收盘高点。这是自 2021 年 11 月以来的最高收盘价，也是连续第四次上涨。


So far this month, the Russell is up 2.5%, outperforming the Nasdaq's 1% gain, the S&P 500's 1.4% climb, and the Dow's 1.7% increase.  
本月到目前为止，罗素指数上涨了 2.5%，跑赢了纳斯达克指数 1% 的涨幅、标准普尔 500 指数 1.4% 的涨幅和道琼斯指数 1.7% 的涨幅。


With the economic growth outlook improving and better visibility into interest-rate cuts, the Russell has rallied off the lows of its rising price channel, says Adam Turnquist, chief technical strategist for LPL Financial. Further, strength in banking stocks, which make up the index's largest sector by weighting, has offered support.  
LPL Financial 首席技术策略师 Adam Turnquist 表示，随着经济增长前景的改善和降息的更好可见性，罗素已经从其不断上涨的价格通道的低点反弹。此外，按权重计算，银行股是该指数中权重最大的板块，其走强也提供了支撑。


"Despite the improving absolute performance, the (Russell) vs. S&P 500 ratio chart still remains in a downtrend and below its declining 200-day moving average," Turnquist said in a report to clients. "A close above the July highs would validate a trend reversal in favor of small caps outperforming."  
“尽管绝对表现有所改善，但（罗素）与标准普尔 500 指数的比率图表仍处于下降趋势中，低于其下降的 200 天移动平均线，”Turnquist 在给客户的一份报告中表示。“收于 7 月高点上方将验证趋势逆转，有利于小盘股跑赢大盘。”


By some measures, 40% of the Russell 2000 index companies on the stock market are not profitable. But while not as abundant as midcaps and big-cap leaders, investors can find some good small-cap choices.  
从某些方面来看，股票市场上 40% 的罗素 2000 指数公司没有盈利。但是，虽然不像中型股和大盘股领导者那样丰富，但投资者可以找到一些不错的小盘股选择。


In the IBD 50, **TG Therapeutics** ([TGTX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TGTX)), with a market cap of $2.79 billion, regained its 50-day moving average. A large number on the list have market caps below $5 billion, including **Harrow** ([HROW](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HROW)), **Atour Lifestyle** ([ATAT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ATAT)), **Vital Farms** ([VITL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=VITL)) and **Travere Therapeutics** ([TVTX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TVTX)).  
在 IBD 50 指数中，市值为 27.9 亿美元的 **TG Therapeutics** （[TGTX）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TGTX) 收复了 50 天移动平均线。名单上有很多公司的市值低于 50 亿美元，包括 **Harrow** （[HROW）、](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HROW)**Atour Lifestyle** （[ATAT）、](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=ATAT)**Vital Farms** （[VITL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=VITL)） 和 **Travere Therapeutics** （[TVTX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TVTX)）。


Also, there's **Argan** ([AGX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AGX)), **Hims & Hers** ([HIMS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HIMS)), **Powell Industries** ([POWL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=POWL)) and **BBB Foods** ([TBBB](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TBBB)), the latter of which was Wednesday's [IBD 50 Stocks to Watch](https://www.investors.com/research/bbb-foods-tbbb-stock-grocery-store/).  
此外，还有**摩洛哥坚果**（[AGX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AGX)），**Hims & Hers**（[HIMS](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HIMS)），**Powell Industries**（[POWL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=POWL)）和**BBB食品**（[TBBB），](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TBBB)后者是周三[值得关注的IBD 50股票](https://www.investors.com/research/bbb-foods-tbbb-stock-grocery-store/)。


Breakouts To Watch 值得关注的突破

In another positive sign, several more high-rated stocks topped buy points in Wednesday's trading, mainly in health care.  
另一个积极的迹象是，在周三的交易中，又有几只高评级股票超过了买盘点，主要是医疗保健。


**United Therapeutics** ([UTHR](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UTHR)) broke out of a flat base in active trading. **Globus Medical** ([GMED](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=GMED)) cleared the 74.21 buy point of another flat base.
