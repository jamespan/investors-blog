  Nasdaq, Nvidia Lead Stock Market Rebound On Easing Recession Fears	纳斯达克和英伟达引领股市反弹，缓解对经济衰退的担忧
date: 2024-08-08 17:37 -0700
author: SCOTT LEHTONEN
---





The Nasdaq composite and Nvidia led the stock market higher Thursday, as Wall Street reacted positively to a bigger-than-expected drop in the Labor Department's initial unemployment claims. That eased recession fears that had gripped Wall Street after weak economic data in recent days.  
纳斯达克综合指数和英伟达指数周四领涨股市，因为华尔街对劳工部首次申请失业救济人数降幅大于预期做出积极反应。这缓解了在最近几天经济数据疲软后笼罩华尔街的经济衰退担忧。




The Dow Jones Industrial Average rallied 1.8%, while the S&P 500 gained 2.3% and the tech-heavy Nasdaq composite advanced 2.9%. For the S&P 500, it was the largest one-day gain since Nov. 30, 2022, per Dow Jones market data. Finally, the small-cap Russell 2000 index climbed 2.4%.  
道琼斯工业平均指数上涨 1.8%，标准普尔 500 指数上涨 2.3%，以科技股为主的纳斯达克综合指数上涨 2.9%。根据道琼斯市场数据，标准普尔 500 指数是自 2022 年 11 月 30 日以来的最大单日涨幅。最后，小盘股罗素 2000 指数上涨 2.4%。


All 11 S&P 500 sectors ended higher, with the technology, communication services, health care, industrials, consumer discretionary and energy sectors each rallying more than 2%. Volume fell on the Nasdaq and the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's session.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块全部收高，其中科技、通信服务、医疗保健、工业、非必需消费品和能源板块均上涨超过 2%。纳斯达克和纽约证券交易所的成交量从周三开始下降。


Gains were broad, with advancers beating decliners by a 4-1 ratio on the NYSE and by nearly 3-to-1 on the Nasdaq, according to FactSet. The Investor S&P 500 Equal Weight ETF ([RSP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RSP)) jumped 1.8%, its best day of the year so far.  
根据 FactSet 的数据，涨幅广泛，纽约证券交易所的涨幅为 4：1，纳斯达克的涨幅接近 3：1。投资者标准普尔 500 等权重 ETF （[RSP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RSP)） 上涨 1.8%，为今年迄今为止最好的一天。


Early Thursday, the Labor Department said that [new claims for unemployment benefits](https://www.investors.com/news/economy/jobless-claims-fall-big-federal-reserve-rate-cut-sp-500/) fell 17,000 to 233,000 in the week through Aug. 3, above economists' 240,000 forecast. The ranks of people continuing to claim benefits rose 6,000 to 1.875 million in the July 27 week.  
周四早些时候，美国劳工部表示，截至 8 月 3 日当周，[新申请失业救济](https://www.investors.com/news/economy/jobless-claims-fall-big-federal-reserve-rate-cut-sp-500/)人数下降 17,000 至 233,000 人，高于经济学家预测的 240,000 人。在 7 月 27 日的一周内，继续申请福利的人数增加了 6,000 人，达到 187.5 万人。


"Initial claims remain more consistent with a mild slowdown than a brewing recession for now," wrote Ian Shepherdson, chief economist at Pantheon Macroeconomics. He noted that the decline in initial claims from the prior week "primarily was driven by claims dropping back in Texas, which bore the brunt of Hurricane Beryl in early July."  
“目前，初请失业金人数仍然更符合温和放缓，而不是正在酝酿中的衰退，”万神殿宏观经济学（Pantheon Macroeconomics）首席经济学家伊恩·谢泼德森（Ian Shepherdson）写道。他指出，初请失业金人数较前一周下降“主要是由于德克萨斯州的申请回落，该州在 7 月初首当其冲地遭受了飓风 Beryl 的袭击。


As of Thursday evening, [markets are pricing in 55.5%](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html) odds of a half-point rate cut by the close of the Sept. 18 Fed meeting, down from 69% on Wednesday and 100% during the height of Monday's market panic.  
截至周四晚间，市场预计美联储在 9 月 18 日会议结束前降息半个基点的可能性为 [55.5%，](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html)低于周三的 69% 和周一市场恐慌高峰期的 100%。
