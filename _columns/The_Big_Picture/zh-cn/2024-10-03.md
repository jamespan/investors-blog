  Stock Market Falls As Wall Street Braces For Crucial Jobs Report	华尔街为关键就业报告做准备，股市下跌
date: 2024-10-03 17:32 -0700
author: SCOTT LEHTONEN

The stock market finished a seesaw session lower Thursday, as Wall Street braced for a September jobs report Friday that could have implications on Fed rate policy.  
股市周四收盘走低，因为华尔街为周五的 9 月就业报告做准备，该报告可能对美联储的利率政策产生影响。




The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 0.4% to hold just above the 42,000 price level. The S&P 500 dipped nearly 0.2%, while the Nasdaq composite moved down a fraction. The small-cap Russell 2000 index lost 0.7% and is now 3.5% off its Sept. 18 high.  
道琼斯工业平均指数下跌 0.4%，略高于 42,000 点价格水平。标准普尔 500 指数下跌近 0.2%，而纳斯达克综合指数下跌一小部分。小盘股罗素 2000 指数下跌 0.7%，目前较 9 月 18 日的高点下跌 3.5%。


Volume dropped on the Nasdaq and the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's session.  
纳斯达克和纽约证券交易所的成交量从周三的交易时段开始下降。


Eight of the 11 S&P 500 sectors ended lower, with consumer discretionary and materials leading the downside. The energy, technology and communication services sectors were the only advancers.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块中有 8 个收低，其中非必需消费品和材料板块领跌。能源、技术和通信服务行业是唯一上涨的板块。


The Aug. 28 distribution day on the Nasdaq and S&P 500 fell off the overall count due to the passage of time, a small dose of bullish news in a mostly quiet session.  
由于时间的流逝，纳斯达克和标准普尔 500 指数 8 月 28 日的发行日从整体计数中下降，在基本平静的交易中出现了少量看涨消息。


The 10-year Treasury yield climbed to its highest level since Sept. 3, closing at 3.85%.  
10 年期美国国债收益率攀升至 9 月 3 日以来的最高水平，收于 3.85%。


The [probability for a 50-basis-point cut](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html?redirect=/trading/interest-rates/countdown-to-fomc.html) in the fed funds rate — which is now at 4.75% to 5% — at the Nov. 7 Federal Open Market Committee meeting eased to 34.6% on Thursday. That's down sharply from 49.3% a week ago. Concurrently, the chances for a quarter-point cut to a range of 4.5%-4.75% ticked higher to 65.4% from 50.7% a week ago.  
在 11 月 7 日的联邦公开市场委员会会议上，联邦基金利率[下调 50 个基点的可能性](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html?redirect=/trading/interest-rates/countdown-to-fomc.html)——目前为 4.75% 至 5%——周四降至 34.6%。这比一周前的 49.3% 大幅下降。与此同时，将四分之一个百分点下调至 4.5%-4.75% 区间的机会从一周前的 50.7% 上升至 65.4%。


Ahead of Friday's stock market open, the Labor Department will release its monthly employment report. The U.S. economy is expected to add 132,500 jobs in September, down from August's 142,000 jobs. The unemployment rate is estimated to hold steady at 4.2%, per Econoday estimates.  
在周五股市开盘前，劳工部将发布其月度就业报告。预计美国经济 9 月份将增加 132,500 个工作岗位，低于 8 月份的 142,000 个工作岗位。根据 Econoday 的估计，失业率估计稳定在 4.2%。


Fed policymakers have said they are keenly watching the labor market as they gauge future interest-rate decisions.  
美联储政策制定者表示，他们在评估未来的利率决策时，正在密切关注劳动力市场。
