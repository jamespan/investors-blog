The stock market closed at session lows Thursday, as the major stock indexes struggled in the wake of Wednesday's Federal Reserve-fueled plunge.  
周四股市收于盘中低点，主要股指在周三美联储推动的暴跌后苦苦挣扎。


The Dow Jones Industrial Average cut gains to less than 0.1%, narrowly breaking a 10-day losing streak. The S&P 500 reversed lower 0.1%. The tech-heavy Nasdaq composite also gave up solid gains to fall 0.1%, as the small cap Russell 2000 index lost 0.5%.  
道琼斯工业平均指数将涨幅降至 0.1% 以下，勉强打破了连续 10 天的下跌。标准普尔 500 指数反转下跌 0.1%。以科技股为主的纳斯达克综合指数也回吐了稳健的涨幅，下跌 0.1%，因为小盘股罗素 2000 指数下跌 0.5%。




Only four of the 11 S&P 500 sectors ended higher, with utilities and financials rising the most. The 10-year Treasury yield climbed to 4.57%. That's its highest level since May 29, per Dow Jones Market Data. The real estate and materials sectors were the day's biggest losers.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块中只有 4 个收高，其中公用事业和金融板块涨幅最大。10 年期美国国债收益率攀升至 4.57%。根据道琼斯市场数据，这是自 5 月 29 日以来的最高水平。房地产和材料板块是当天最大的输家。


Volume fell on the Nasdaq and the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's busy session.  
纳斯达克和纽约证券交易所的成交量从周三的繁忙交易时段开始下降。


Up next, Wall Street will shift its attention to Friday's personal income and outlays report. That report includes the core PCE price index, which is the Federal Reserve's favored inflation reading. The core PCE price index is expected to rise 0.2% for the month of November, with an annual increase of 2.9%, per Econoday.  
接下来，华尔街将把注意力转移到周五的个人收入和支出报告上。该报告包括核心 PCE 价格指数，这是美联储青睐的通胀读数。据 Econoday 报道，预计 11 月核心 PCE 价格指数将上涨 0.2%，年涨幅为 2.9%。

Stock market leaders to watch include **Alphabet** ([GOOGL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=GOOGL)), **Meta Platforms** ([META](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=META)) and **Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing** ([TSM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSM)).  
值得关注的股市领导者包括 **Alphabet** （[GOOGL](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=GOOGL)）、**Meta Platforms** （[META）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=META) 和**台积电** （[TSM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSM)）。



Google parent Alphabet moved up 0.1% after trimming a 2.4% gain. Shares are back in the buy range from a cup-with-handle's 182.49 buy point, per [IBD MarketSurge chart analysis.](https://get.investors.com/marketsurge/?artProdLink=MarketSurge) But it's risky to buy as a stock trends lower.  
谷歌母公司 Alphabet 在收窄了 2.4% 的涨幅后上涨 0.1%。根据 [IBD MarketSurge 图表分析](https://get.investors.com/marketsurge/?artProdLink=MarketSurge)，股价从带把手杯的 182.49 买入点回到买入区间。但是，随着股票走低，买入是有风险的。


Shares of Meta closed further below their flat base's 602.95 buy point, after a 0.3% decline. Wait for a decisive retake of that entry before considering a new purchase of shares.  
Meta 的股价在下跌 0.3% 后进一步低于其平底的 602.95 买入点。等待决定性地重新获得该条目，然后再考虑购买新的股票。


Taiwan Semiconductor, better known as TSMC, closed right at its 50-day line, about 5% away from a 205.63 cup-with-handle buy point.  
台积电（Taiwan Semiconductor），更广为人知的名字是台积电（TSMC），收于其 5 日线，距离 205.63 的带手柄杯买入点约 5%。
