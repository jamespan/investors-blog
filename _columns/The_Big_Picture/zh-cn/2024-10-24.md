Tech stocks rebounded Thursday, fueled by upbeat results from **Tesla** ([TSLA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSLA)) and bullish comments from the electric car maker's chief executive, Elon Musk. Tesla stock surged nearly 22%, its largest percent increase since May 9, 2013, according to Dow Jones market data.  
科技股周四反弹，受**特斯拉** （[TSLA）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSLA) 乐观业绩和这家电动汽车制造商首席执行官埃隆马斯克 （Elon Musk） 的看涨言论的推动。根据道琼斯市场数据，特斯拉股价飙升近 22%，这是自 2013 年 5 月 9 日以来的最大百分比涨幅。


The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 0.3%, while the S&P 500 rose 0.2%. The Nasdaq composite ended the day up 0.8%, near session highs. And the small-cap Russell 2000 index inched higher.  
道琼斯工业平均指数下跌 0.3%，而标准普尔 500 指数上涨 0.2%。纳斯达克综合指数当天收盘上涨 0.8%，接近盘中高点。小盘股罗素 2000 指数小幅走高。




Volume fell on the Nasdaq, but inched higher on the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's session.  
纳斯达克的成交量下降，但纽约证券交易所的成交量较周三小幅上涨。


Six of the 11 S&P 500 sectors ended lower, with materials and industrials leading the downside. The consumer discretionary sector was the clear leader Thursday.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块中有 6 个收低，其中材料和工业板块领跌。周四，非必需消费品板块明显领先。


On the economic front, the Labor Department's first-time jobless claims unexpectedly fell to 227,000 vs. 241,000 in the previous week. They were anticipated to rise to 247,000, per Econoday estimates, due to an expected rise in filings following the recent hurricanes.  
在经济方面，美国劳工部首次申请失业救济人数意外降至 227,000 人，而前一周为 241,000 人。根据 Econoday 的估计，预计申请量将上升到 247,000 份，因为预计最近的飓风过后申请量会增加。

Tesla Stock Surges 特斯拉股票飙升
--------------------------


Late Wednesday, Tesla announced a surprise gain in third-quarter earnings, with quarterly profit growing 9%, capping off a big month of announcements for the EV giant. Revenue came in light. Still, Tesla stock bullishly regained its 50-day line Thursday, approaching a 264.86 buy point in a cup with handle.  
周三晚些时候，特斯拉宣布第三季度收益意外增长，季度利润增长 9%，为这家电动汽车巨头一个月的重大公告画上了句号。收入出现了。尽管如此，特斯拉股票周四仍看涨收复了 50 日线，在 cup with handle。


On the [Tesla earnings call](https://www.investors.com/news/tesla-stock-earnings-q3-elon-musk/), CEO Musk was calmly bullish throughout, upbeat on full self-driving, vehicle deliveries, storage and more.  
在[特斯拉财报电话会议上](https://www.investors.com/news/tesla-stock-earnings-q3-elon-musk/)，首席执行官马斯克自始至终都平静地看好，对全自动驾驶、车辆交付、存储等方面持乐观态度。


Meanwhile, a number of analyst firms — including Goldman Sachs, Bank of America and Wells Fargo — [increased Tesla stock price targets](https://www.investors.com/news/tesla-stock-early-christmas-present-earnings-brings-out-bulls/) following the company's third-quarter earnings Wednesday, which contained profit and margins surprises.  
与此同时，包括高盛（Goldman Sachs）、美国银行（Bank of America）和富国银行（Wells Fargo）在内的一些分析公司在特斯拉周三公布第三季度财报后[上调了特斯拉的目标价](https://www.investors.com/news/tesla-stock-early-christmas-present-earnings-brings-out-bulls/)，其中包括利润和利润率意外。


Wedbush Securities analyst Dan Ives, a longtime Tesla bull, wrote that the company "delivered an early Christmas present for investors as the bulls got a monster margin rebound and a surprisingly strong delivery outlook for 2025."  
Wedbush Securities 分析师丹·艾夫斯 （Dan Ives） 是特斯拉的长期看涨者，他写道，该公司“为投资者提前送上了一份圣诞礼物，因为多头的利润率大幅反弹，2025 年的交付前景出人意料地强劲。
