  Stock Market Pauses Even As Nvidia Hits All-Time High; Netflix Jumps On Earnings Beat	即使 Nvidia 创下历史新高，股市也暂停;Netflix 的收益超出预期

The stock market rally paused Thursday, as Wall Street initially climbed on better-than-expected U.S. retail sales and unemployment data but faded into the close. **Nvidia** ([NVDA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVDA)) hit a record high, propelled by [strong results](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/tsm-stock-taiwan-semiconductor-q3-2024-earnings/) from **Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing** ([TSM](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSM)).  
股市涨势周四暂停，因为华尔街最初因美国零售销售和失业数据好于预期而攀升，但在收盘时逐渐消失。**英伟达** （[NVDA）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVDA) 创下历史新高，受**台积电** （[TSM）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TSM) [强劲业绩](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/tsm-stock-taiwan-semiconductor-q3-2024-earnings/)的推动。


And late Thursday, **Netflix** ([NFLX](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NFLX)) rallied nearly 4% on a [strong third-quarter earnings report](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/netflix-stock-q3-2024-earnings-nflx-stock/), sparking a modest rise in overnight tech futures.  
周四晚些时候，**Netflix** （[NFLX）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NFLX) 因[强劲的第三季度收益报告](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/netflix-stock-q3-2024-earnings-nflx-stock/)上涨近 4%，引发隔夜科技期货小幅上涨。




The Dow Jones Industrial Average climbed 0.4% and held most of the day's gains, reaching another record closing high. The S&P 500 ended one point lower after giving up moderate gains.  
道琼斯工业平均指数上涨 0.4%，守住了当天的大部分涨幅，再次创下收盘新高。标准普尔 500 指数在回吐温和上涨后收盘下跌 1 点。


The Nasdaq composite closed less than 0.1% higher after being up nearly 1% earlier in the session. The small-cap Russell 2000 index declined nearly 0.3% to end a four-day win streak.  
纳斯达克综合指数在盘中早些时候上涨近 1% 后收盘上涨不到 0.1%。小盘股罗素 2000 指数下跌近 0.3%，结束了连续四天的上涨。


Volume rose on the Nasdaq but edged lower on the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's session. The Nasdaq suffered a rare form of distribution, called a [stalling day](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stalling-signals-an-early-warning-for-stock-market-heres-how-to-find-this-insidious-signal/#:~:text=You%20may%20have%20heard%20the,gain%20by%20the%20day's%20close.). That's when an index closes higher but gives back the majority of its intraday gain by the day's close. That takes the Nasdaq's distribution-day count to five.  
纳斯达克的成交量上升，但纽约证券交易所的成交量从周三开始小幅走低。纳斯达克遭遇了一种罕见的派息形式，称为[停滞日](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stalling-signals-an-early-warning-for-stock-market-heres-how-to-find-this-insidious-signal/#:~:text=You%20may%20have%20heard%20the,gain%20by%20the%20day's%20close.)。那是指数收盘走高但在当天收盘前回吐大部分盘中涨幅的时候。这使得纳斯达克的分销日数达到 5 天。


Six of the 11 S&P 500 sectors ended lower, with utilities and communication services leading the downside. The energy and technology sector were the leaders Thursday.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块中有 6 个收低，其中公用事业和通信服务领跌。能源和科技板块周四领涨。


In economic news, retail sales rose 0.4% in September, topping expectations of a 0.3% increase, according to the Econoday consensus forecast. August's gain was unrevised at 0.1%. Excluding autos, sales in September climbed 0.5%, above 0.1% forecasts. Factoring out both autos and gas, sales rose 0.7%, ahead of 0.3% estimates. August's gain factoring out autos and gas was revised up slightly to 0.3%.  
经济新闻方面，根据 Econoday 的一致预测，9 月份零售额增长 0.4%，高于 0.3% 的预期。8 月份的涨幅未修正，为 0.1%。不包括汽车，9 月份的销量增长 0.5%，高于 0.1% 的预期。剔除汽车和汽油，销售额增长 0.7%，高于 0.3% 的预期。剔除汽车和天然气的 8 月份涨幅略微上调至 0.3%。


New claims for unemployment benefits unexpectedly fell 19,000 to 241,000 in the week ended Oct. 12, despite the hit from Hurricane Milton. That likely reflects a delay in people filing for benefits. So claims could spike next week. Wall Street had expected 260,000 new claims, up 2,000 from the prior week's initially reported figure. Initial claims in Florida fell by 3,428.  
尽管受到飓风米尔顿的袭击，但在截至 10 月 12 日的一周内，新申请失业救济人数意外下降 19,000 至 241,000 人。这可能反映了人们申请福利的延迟。因此，下周申请失业救济金人数可能会飙升。华尔街此前预计新增申请失业救济人数为 260,000 人，比前一周最初报告的数字增加了 2,000 人。佛罗里达州的初请失业金人数减少了 3,428 人。
