  Tech Stocks Lead Stock Market Lower As Wall Street Braces For Powell Speech	科技股引领股市走低，华尔街为鲍威尔演讲做准备
date: 2024-08-22 17:23 -0700
author: SCOTT LEHTONEN
---





The stock market rally took a step backward on Thursday as Wall Street braces for Fed Chairman Jerome Powell's pivotal speech from Jackson Hole, Wyo., Friday morning.  
周四股市反弹倒退一步，因为华尔街为美联储主席杰罗姆鲍威尔周五上午在怀俄明州杰克逊霍尔的关键讲话做准备。




The Dow Jones Industrial Average dropped 0.4%, while the S&P 500 lost 0.9% and the tech-heavy Nasdaq composite slid 1.7%. For the Nasdaq, it was the largest one-day percentage loss since Aug. 5, per Dow Jones market data. The small-cap Russell 2000 index fell nearly 1%.  
道琼斯工业平均指数下跌 0.4%，而标准普尔 500 指数下跌 0.9%，以科技股为主的纳斯达克综合指数下跌 1.7%。根据道琼斯市场数据，纳斯达克指数创下自 8 月 5 日以来的最大单日百分比跌幅。小盘股罗素 2000 指数下跌近 1%。


Eight of the 11 S&P 500 sectors ended lower, with technology and consumer discretionary the biggest losers. The technology sector index fell 2.3%, its worst day since Aug. 5. On the upside, the real estate, financial and energy sectors held modest gains.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块中有 8 个收低，其中科技和非必需消费品板块跌幅最大。科技板块指数下跌 2.3%，为 8 月 5 日以来最糟糕的一天。从好的方面来看，房地产、金融和能源板块小幅上涨。


Volume rose on the Nasdaq but fell on the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's session. That resulted in the first [distribution day](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-timing-the-subtle-sign-of-market-tops/) for the Nasdaq since the follow-through day on Aug. 13.  
纳斯达克的成交量从周三开始上升，但纽约证券交易所的成交量下降。这导致纳斯达克自 8 月 13 日后续日以来的第一个[派发日](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-timing-the-subtle-sign-of-market-tops/)。


Losses were broad, with decliners outnumbering advancers by a nearly 2-1 ratio on the NYSE and by more than 2-to-1 on the Nasdaq, according to [MarketWatch](https://www.marketwatch.com/market-data/us). The Invesco S&P 500 Equal Weight ETF ([RSP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RSP)) only fell 0.3%, a mild loss compared with the Nasdaq and S&P 500, indicating that heavier losses occurred in the higher-weighted stocks.  
根据 [MarketWatch](https://www.marketwatch.com/market-data/us) 的数据，损失范围很广，纽约证券交易所的下跌者数量与上涨者的数量之比接近 2：1，纳斯达克的跌幅超过 2：1。景顺标准普尔 500 等权重 ETF （[RSP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=RSP)） 仅下跌 0.3%，与纳斯达克和标准普尔 500 指数相比略有损失，表明权重较高的股票损失更大。


The 10-year Treasury yield rose eight basis points to 3.86%.  
10 年期美国国债收益率上涨 8 个基点至 3.86%。


Early Friday, [Powell will likely say](https://www.investors.com/news/economy/federal-reserve-chairman-jerome-powell-jackson-hole-bls-job-revisions-sp-500/) at his Jackson Hole speech that inflation has eased to the point where the risks are now tilted toward waiting too long to cut rates. Although the effects of Hurricane Beryl contributed to a soft July jobs report, hiring momentum has clearly slowed.  
周五早些时候，[鲍威尔可能会](https://www.investors.com/news/economy/federal-reserve-chairman-jerome-powell-jackson-hole-bls-job-revisions-sp-500/)在杰克逊霍尔演讲中表示，通胀已经缓解到现在风险倾向于等待太久才降息的程度。尽管飓风 Beryl 的影响导致 7 月就业报告疲软，但招聘势头明显放缓。

What To Do Now 现在该做什么
---------------------


The Nasdaq is right back at its 50-day moving average following Thursday's sharp loss. A sharp breach of that level would likely indicate further weakness, while a rebound would be bullish for the market's short-term prospects. And Friday's Powell speech could be the catalyst in either direction.  
纳斯达克指数在周四大幅下跌后回到了 50 天移动平均线。大幅突破该水平可能预示着市场进一步疲软，而反弹则对市场的短期前景有利。周五的鲍威尔讲话可能成为任何一个方向的催化剂。


Still, it's normal for the market to pull back after a sharp rally since the Aug. 5 market low, so IBD is maintaining its recommended exposure level at 60% to 80%.  
尽管如此，自 8 月 5 日市场低点以来，市场在大幅反弹后回落是正常的，因此 IBD 将其建议的风险敞口维持在 60% 至 80%。


This environment clearly puts investor emphasis on [risk management](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/risk-management-in-the-stock-market-how-much-money-to-invest-now/), one of the [four pillars of The IBD Methodology](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-investing-ibd-methodology/). That means that incrementally raising exposure remains a priority. See how your existing positions and the latest buys perform before putting more capital to work.  
这种环境显然使投资者重视[风险管理](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/risk-management-in-the-stock-market-how-much-money-to-invest-now/)，这是 [IBD 方法论的四大支柱](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-investing-ibd-methodology/)之一。这意味着逐步提高曝光率仍然是一个优先事项。在投入更多资金之前，先了解您现有的头寸和最新的买入情况。


If you're making progress, then it makes sense to add more exposure. If not, then hold off until you can find more opportunities.  
如果你正在取得进展，那么增加更多的曝光是有意义的。如果没有，那就等到你能找到更多的机会。
