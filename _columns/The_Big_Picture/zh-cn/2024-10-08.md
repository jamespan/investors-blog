  Nasdaq Leads Another Stock Market Rally, Eyes Breakout; Nvidia Extends Win Streak	纳斯达克领涨股市再度上涨，眼前突破;Nvidia 延续连胜纪录

The Nasdaq composite and S&P 500 outperformed in the stock market Tuesday as oil prices plunged and the 10-year Treasury yield slowed its ascent, holding just above the 4% level.  
纳斯达克综合指数和标准普尔 500 指数周二在股市中表现出色，因为油价暴跌，10 年期美国国债收益率放缓上涨速度，保持在 4% 的水平上方。


The Nasdaq jumped nearly 1.5% in higher volume, picture-perfect price action for the bulls after the index marked a [distribution day](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-spot-stock-market-tops-track-the-distribution-days/) Monday. The Nasdaq is back above the 18,000 level with its sights now set on its late-September high of 18,327. A decisive move above this level would qualify as a bullish breakout.  
纳斯达克指数上涨近 1.5%，成交量更高，在周一该指数标志着[分配日](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-spot-stock-market-tops-track-the-distribution-days/)后，多头的价格走势完美无缺。纳斯达克指数回到 18,000 点上方，现在将目光投向 9 月下旬的高点 18,327 点。决定性地高于该水平将符合看涨突破的条件。




The Nasdaq continues to perform well amid mild signs of institutional selling. Of the Nasdaq's five distribution days, only two show declines of 1% or more. Despite Tuesday's solid gain, Nasdaq breadth was relatively weak, with decliners having a slight edge over advancers.  
纳斯达克指数在机构抛售的温和迹象中继续表现良好。在纳斯达克的五个分销日中，只有两个日的跌幅为 1% 或更多。尽管周二强劲上涨，但纳斯达克指数的广度相对较弱，下跌的股票略高于上涨的股票。


Stock Market Today 今日股市
-----------------------


**Nvidia** ([NVDA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVDA)) was a top gainer in the Nasdaq 100, up 4%. The stock broke out of a downtrend this week, albeit in light volume. Fellow AI stock **Broadcom** ([AVGO](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AVGO)) also outperformed with its 3% gain. Broadcom rallied in light volume, although volume was up from Monday. Broadcom closed just above a handle entry of 180.25.  
**英伟达** （[NVDA）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=NVDA) 是纳斯达克 100 指数中涨幅最大的股票，上涨 4%。该股本周突破了下跌趋势，尽管成交量不大。另一只 AI 股票**博通** （[AVGO）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=AVGO) 也以 3% 的涨幅跑赢大盘。博通 （Broadcom） 以低价上涨，尽管交易量比周一有所上升。博通收盘价略高于 180.25 的收盘价。


**![](https://www.investors.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/MP100824_2-210x300.jpg)Palo Alto Networks** ([PANW](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=PANW)) also moved well in the Nasdaq 100, up 5.1% in higher volume. The security software stock broke out over a [trendline](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-draw-trend-lines-and-find-lower-buy-points-in-stock-charts/) and cleared the 350 level. Palo Alto Networks shows some tight weekly closes near its 10-week line as it sets up in a base with an entry of 375.37. The company isn't the growth engine it once was, but it's still widely regarded as an institutional-quality name in the group.  
**![](https://www.investors.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/MP100824_2-210x300.jpg)Palo Alto Networks** （[PANW](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=PANW)） 在纳斯达克 100 指数中也表现良好，成交量上涨 5.1%。安全软件股突破[趋势线](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/how-to-draw-trend-lines-and-find-lower-buy-points-in-stock-charts/)并突破 350 水平。Palo Alto Networks 在其 10 周线附近显示一些紧凑的每周收盘价，因为它以 375.37 的入场价为基础。该公司已不再是以前的增长引擎，但它仍然被广泛认为是集团中的机构级品牌。


**MercadoLibre** ([MELI](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MELI)) rallied nearly 3% after finding support at its 50-day moving average. But the stock closed just a hair below its 10-week line on the weekly chart.  
**MercadoLibre** （[MELI）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MELI) 在其 3 天移动平均线获得支撑后上涨近 50%。但该股收盘价仅比周线上的 10 周线低一点。


The S&P 500 added 1% in lower volume, while the Dow Jones Industrial Average and Russell 2000 small-cap index lagged with gains of 0.3% and 0.1%, respectively.  
标准普尔 500 指数在成交量较低的情况下上涨了 1%，而道琼斯工业平均指数和罗素 2000 小盘股指数分别上涨了 0.3% 和 0.1%。


Breadth was barely positive on the NYSE as sellers hit oil and gas, mining and steel stocks. Just after the stock market close, West Texas intermediate crude oil futures were trading around $74 a barrel, down 4%, after hitting an intraday low of $72.69.  
纽约证券交易所的广度几乎没有乐观，因为卖家打击了石油和天然气、采矿和钢铁股。股市收盘后，西德克萨斯中质原油期货在触及盘中低点 72.69 美元后，交易价格约为每桶 74 美元，下跌 4%。


China Stocks Weaken 中国股市走弱
--------------------------


Overall, it was another strong performance for the stock market despite broad-based selling in Chinese stocks. After soaring nearly 22% two weeks ago, and 6.7% last week, **Alibaba** ([BABA](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BABA)) pared losses but still fell nearly 7%, while [IBD Sector Leader](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/sector-leaders) **Futu** ([FUTU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FUTU)) plunged 13%, snapping an 11-session win streak.  
总体而言，尽管中国股市广泛抛售，但这是股市的又一次强劲表现。在两周前飙升近 22% 和上周飙升 6.7% 之后，**阿里巴巴** （[BABA）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=BABA) 收复失地但仍下跌近 7%，而 [IBD 行业领导者](https://research.investors.com/stock-lists/sector-leaders)**富途** （[FUTU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=FUTU)） 暴跌 13%，结束了连续 11 个交易日的胜利。


Sentiment was negative in Chinese stocks after China's top economic planning agency, in a highly anticipated briefing, didn't announce any new details regarding recently announced stimulus measures.  
在中国最高经济规划机构在备受期待的简报会上没有宣布有关最近宣布的刺激措施的任何新细节后，中国股市的情绪消极。


Some key economic headlines are on the horizon, including the release of the minutes from the Sept. 18 Federal Reserve meeting, due Wednesday at 2 p.m. ET. After that, the latest reading on consumer prices will be out Thursday at 8:30 a.m. ET. The producer price index, a measure of wholesale inflation, comes out Friday.  
一些重要的经济头条新闻即将出现，包括将于美国东部时间周三下午 2 点公布的 9 月 18 日美联储会议纪要。在此之后，消费者价格的最新数据将于美国东部时间周四上午 8：30 公布。衡量批发通胀的生产者价格指数将于周五公布。

In light of the strong jobs report Friday, the market has dialed back rate-cut expectations for this year. For now, [CME FedWatch](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html) expects a quarter-point cut at the November meeting, and another one at the December meeting. After soaring 23 basis points last week, the 10-year Treasury yield traded up to 4.06% Wednesday before backing off highs.  
鉴于周五强劲的就业报告，市场已经下调了今年的降息预期。目前，[CME FedWatch](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html) 预计 11 月会议将降息 25 个基点，并在 12 月会议上再次降息。在上周飙升 23 个基点后，10 年期美国国债收益率周三上涨至 4.06%，随后回落至高点。


The stock market continues to perform quite well even as interest rates and the U.S. dollar rally. The Invesco DB U.S. Dollar Bullish Fund ([UUP](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UUP)) continues to hold gains after soaring above all of its moving averages last week.  
即使在利率和美元上涨的情况下，股市仍然表现良好。Invesco DB 美元看涨基金 （[UUP）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=UUP) 在上周飙升至所有移动平均线上方后继续保持上涨。


Market Movers 市场推动者
-------------------


Leadership has broadened out considerably. Online broker **Robinhood** ([HOOD](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HOOD)) broke out over an alternate handle entry of 24.13. Volume was very heavy, unlike Robinhood's first breakout over a 21.54 handle entry last month.  
领导力已经大大扩大。在线经纪人 **Robinhood** （[HOOD）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=HOOD) 因 24.13 的备用句柄条目而爆发。成交量非常大，这与 Robinhood 上个月首次突破 21.54 关口不同。


**Docusign** ([DOCU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=DOCU)) gapped up to a 52-week high on news it's replacing **MDU Resources** ([MDU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MDU)) in the S&P MidCap 400. The breakout comes after the e-signature firm cleared a 61.09 buy point in late September.  
**Docusign** （[DOCU）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=DOCU) 跳空升至 52 周高点，此前有消息称它将取代标准普尔中型股 400 中的 **MDU Resources** （[MDU](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MDU)）。这家电子签名公司在 9 月下旬清除了 61.09 的买入点后取得了突破。


**Commvault Systems** ([CVLT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CVLT)), meanwhile, cleared a flat base with a 157.13 buy point on news the company's data protection solutions will be available on Amazon Web Services.  
与此同时，**Commvault Systems** （[CVLT）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=CVLT) 以 157.13 的买入点清除了平坦的基础，因为有消息称该公司的数据保护解决方案将在 Amazon Web Services 上提供。


In the pollution control group, **Tetra Tech** ([TTEK](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TTEK)) reclaimed a 48.26 entry with conviction, rising 3.5% in strong trade.  
在污染控制组中，**Tetra Tech** （[TTEK）](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=TTEK) 以 48.26 的成绩重新获得 3.5% 的强劲交易。
