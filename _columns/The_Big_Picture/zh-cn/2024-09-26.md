With a critical inflation report due Friday morning, traders still sent the S&P 500 to a new high Thursday. And the Nasdaq composite remains at its highest level since it touched a record on July 16.  
周五上午将发布关键的通胀报告，交易员周四仍将标准普尔 500 指数推至新高。纳斯达克综合指数仍处于 7 月 16 日触及历史新高以来的最高水平。




The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 0.6% to rebound from Wednesday's bearish downside reversal. The S&P 500 gained 0.4%, while the Nasdaq composite moved up 0.6%. The small-cap Russell 2000 index climbed 0.6%.  
道琼斯工业平均指数上涨 0.6%，从周三的看跌下行逆转中反弹。标准普尔 500 指数上涨 0.4%，而纳斯达克综合指数上涨 0.6%。小盘股罗素 2000 指数上涨 0.6%。


Seven of the 11 S&P 500 sectors ended higher, with materials and technology leading the way. The energy and real estate sectors paced the losers.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块中有 7 个板块收高，其中材料和科技领涨。能源和房地产板块紧随跌幅。


Volume rose on the Nasdaq and the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's session.  
纳斯达克和纽约证券交易所的成交量从周三开始上升。


Ahead of Friday's stock market open, the Commerce Department will release its monthly personal income and outlays report for August. Personal income is expected to rise 0.4% on the month. The core personal consumption expenditures, or PCE, price index is estimated to increase 0.2% for the month, with an annual rise of 2.7%.  
在周五股市开盘前，美国商务部将发布 8 月份的月度个人收入和支出报告。预计个人收入当月将增长 0.4%。核心个人消费支出 （PCE） 价格指数估计当月上涨 0.2%，年涨幅为 2.7%。


The core PCE price index is the Federal Reserve's favored inflation gauge.  
核心 PCE 价格指数是美联储青睐的通胀指标。

The S&P 500 is close to [triggering a power trend](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-condition-called-power-trend-is-when-you-really-need-to-be-buying/), while the [Nasdaq entered one today](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/dow-jones-futures-market-rally-power-trend-ai-stocks-super-micro-dive/). That adds a layer of bullishness to an already positive market. A power trend is a part of [IBD's Market School](https://get.investors.com/online-courses/market-school/) trading rules and represents unusually bullish trading conditions.  
标准普尔 500 指数接近触发力量趋势，而[纳斯达克指数今天进入](https://www.investors.com/market-trend/stock-market-today/dow-jones-futures-market-rally-power-trend-ai-stocks-super-micro-dive/)[了力量趋势](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-condition-called-power-trend-is-when-you-really-need-to-be-buying/)。这为本已积极的市场增添了一层看涨情绪。力量趋势是 [IBD 市场学校](https://get.investors.com/online-courses/market-school/)交易规则的一部分，代表异常看涨的交易条件。


Amid the ongoing stock market rally, IBD has an 80%-100% recommended exposure threshold, which means that investors should be heavily invested. Investors who need to add more exposure should look for market leaders breaking out past correct entries.

But investors shouldn't become complacent. The current environment still requires [risk management](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/risk-management-in-the-stock-market-how-much-money-to-invest-now/), one of the [four pillars of The IBD Methodology](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-investing-ibd-methodology/). That means incrementally adding and reducing exposure to stay in sync with the broad market. That means identifying sell signals and other abrupt character changes, like **MakeMyTrip** ([MMYT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MMYT)), which plunged more than 11% Thursday.  
但投资者不应该自满。当前环境仍然需要[风险管理](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/risk-management-in-the-stock-market-how-much-money-to-invest-now/)，这是 [IBD 方法的四大支柱](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-investing-ibd-methodology/)之一。这意味着逐步增加和减少风险敞口，以与大盘保持同步。这意味着识别卖出信号和其他突然的特征变化，例如 **MakeMyTrip** （[MMYT](https://research.investors.com/quote.aspx?symbol=MMYT)），周四暴跌超过 11%。
