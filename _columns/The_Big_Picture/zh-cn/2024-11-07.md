The S&P 500 and the Nasdaq composite rallied Thursday as Wall Street reacted to the Federal Reserve's widely expected interest-rate cut and comments from Fed Chair Jerome Powell.  
标准普尔 500 指数和纳斯达克综合指数周四上涨，因为华尔街对美联储普遍预期的降息和美联储主席杰罗姆鲍威尔的评论做出了反应。


But the Dow Jones Industrial Average inched lower after hitting another record high. The S&P 500 climbed 0.7% and the Nasdaq composite advanced 1.5%. Both also posted new highs and made most of their gains before the Fed announcement. The small-cap Russell 2000 index fell 0.4%.  
但道琼斯工业平均指数在创下新高后小幅走低。标准普尔 500 指数上涨 0.7%，纳斯达克综合指数上涨 1.5%。两者都创下了新高，并在美联储宣布之前获得了大部分涨幅。小盘股罗素 2000 指数下跌 0.4%。




Volume rose on the Nasdaq, but fell on the New York Stock Exchange from Wednesday's session. The Nasdaq's more than 9 million shares were the most since the Sept. 20 triple witching session and the fourth largest total this year.  
纳斯达克的成交量上升，但纽约证券交易所的成交量从周三开始下跌。纳斯达克指数超过 900 万股是自 9 月 20 日三重波动以来的最高水平，也是今年第四高的总量。


Eight of the 11 S&P 500 sectors ended higher, with consumer discretionary and communication services leading the upside. The financial sector was the clear loser Thursday, as the 10-year Treasury yield fell to 4.34%.  
标准普尔 500 指数的 11 个板块中有 8 个板块收高，其中非必需消费品和通信服务领涨。周四，金融板块显然是输家，因为 10 年期美国国债收益率跌至 4.34%。


The [Federal Reserve cut its main policy rate](https://www.investors.com/news/economy/federal-reserve-rate-cut-jerome-powell-trump-election-cuts-sp-500/) by 25 basis points on Thursday, citing a continuation of slower job gains. That placed the target rate at 4.5% to 4.75%. And Powell left the door open for more rate cuts in the future, as Wall Street expects.  
[美联储周四将其主要政策利率下调](https://www.investors.com/news/economy/federal-reserve-rate-cut-jerome-powell-trump-election-cuts-sp-500/) 25 个基点，理由是就业增长持续放缓。这使目标利率为 4.5% 至 4.75%。正如华尔街所预期的那样，鲍威尔为未来进一步降息敞开了大门。


Regarding a December rate cut, Powell told reporters, "We think that even with today's cut, policy is still restrictive."  
对于 12 月降息，鲍威尔告诉记者，“我们认为，即使今天降息，政策仍然具有限制性。


Powell continued, "We're on a path to a more neutral stance." That makes it clear that more rate cuts are on the way, though Powell wouldn't commit to the timing of those cuts.  
鲍威尔继续说，“我们正走在一条更加中立立场的道路上。这清楚地表明，更多的降息即将到来，尽管鲍威尔不会承诺降息的时间。

What Investors Should Do Now投资者现在应该做什么
--------------------------------------


![](https://www.investors.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/MP-110724-216x300.jpg)Following Wednesday's powerful advance, IBD upped its recommended exposure from 60%-to-80% to 80%-to-100% to reflect the strengthening action in the major stock indexes. And stocks continued to post more all-time highs Thursday.  
继周三的强劲上涨之后，IBD 将其建议的敞口从 60% 至 80% 上调至 80% 至 100%，以反映主要股指的走强行动。周四，股市继续创下更多历史新高。


Despite the bullishness, the stock market environment requires diligent [risk management](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/risk-management-in-the-stock-market-how-much-money-to-invest-now/), one of the [four pillars of The IBD Methodology](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-investing-ibd-methodology/). Following this week's sharp gains, many leading stocks are already extended beyond correct entries, so it's important to remain disciplined and not chase them. Be sure to buy stocks that are in proper buy areas, mainly within 5% of their buy points.  
尽管看涨，但股市环境需要勤奋的[风险管理](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/risk-management-in-the-stock-market-how-much-money-to-invest-now/)，这是 [IBD 方法论的四大支柱](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-investing-ibd-methodology/)之一。继本周的大幅上涨之后，许多领先股票已经延伸到正确入场之外，因此保持纪律而不是追逐它们很重要。请务必购买位于适当购买区域的股票，主要是在其购买点数的 5% 以内。


Amid the powerful gains, the question now will be whether these stocks can hold their gains and show continued strength, or if they'll pull back sharply.  
在强劲的收益中，现在的问题是这些股票是否能够守住涨幅并表现出持续的强劲势头，或者它们是否会大幅回落。


The Nasdaq's remaining two [distribution days](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-timing-the-subtle-sign-of-market-tops/) dropped off due to 5% gains from those sessions, while the S&P 500's Oct. 7 day fell off for the same reason. When the index advances 5% from the closing price of the distribution day, it is considered no longer relevant to the current market outlook.  
纳斯达克剩余的[两个派息日](https://www.investors.com/how-to-invest/investors-corner/stock-market-timing-the-subtle-sign-of-market-tops/)因这些交易日上涨 5% 而下跌，而标准普尔 500 指数 10 月 7 日则因同样的原因下跌。当指数从分配日的收盘价上涨 5% 时，它被认为不再与当前的市场前景相关。
